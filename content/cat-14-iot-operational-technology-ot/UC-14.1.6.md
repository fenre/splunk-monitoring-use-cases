# UC-14.1.6 · Environmental Compliance

## Description

Temperature/humidity exceedances in data centers risk equipment damage; in labs they invalidate experiments. Compliance monitoring is mandatory.

## Value

Temperature/humidity exceedances in data centers risk equipment damage; in labs they invalidate experiments. Compliance monitoring is mandatory.

## Implementation

Deploy environmental sensors per ASHRAE guidelines. Ingest via SNMP or MQTT. Alert immediately on out-of-range conditions. Log compliance data for audit. Track seasonal patterns for cooling optimization.

| where temp_f > 80 OR temp_f < 64 OR humidity_pct > 60 OR humidity_pct < 40
| table _time, zone, sensor, temp_f, humidity_pct
