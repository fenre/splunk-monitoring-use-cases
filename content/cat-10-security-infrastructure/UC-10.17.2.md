# UC-10.17.2 · SonicWall Geo-IP Block and Country-Deny Audit (SonicWall)

## Description

Geo-blocking stops opportunistic scans and complies with data-residency policies. Auditing denies proves the control is active and highlights mis-tuned categories.

## Value

Demonstrates enforcement to regulators and helps tune exceptions before business traffic is impacted.

## Implementation

Confirm Geo-IP filtering is enabled and logged with country fields. Normalize ISO country codes; enrich with a risk lookup for embargoed regions.

## SPL

```spl
index=netfw sourcetype="dell:sonicwall:firewall" earliest=-24h
| where match(lower(fw_action),"(?i)block|deny|drop") AND (isnotnull(src_country) OR match(_raw,"(?i)geo"))
| stats count by src_country, dst_country, rule
| sort -count
```

## Visualization

Choropleth or bar chart (top blocked countries), timechart (geo deny rate).
