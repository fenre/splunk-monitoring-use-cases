# UC-13.2.32 · ITSI Team Permission and RBAC Audit

## Description

ITSI team assignments control service visibility and episode access. Auditing permissions ensures least-privilege access and prevents unauthorized service modifications.

## Value

ITSI team assignments control service visibility and episode access. Auditing permissions ensures least-privilege access and prevents unauthorized service modifications.

## Implementation

Define ITSI teams aligned to organizational structure. Assign services to teams for scoped visibility. Audit team membership quarterly — remove departed users, verify role assignments (itoa_admin, itoa_team_admin, itoa_analyst, itoa_user). Ensure admin role inherits itoa_admin in authorize.conf. Monitor for users with excessive permissions.
