# UC-21.3.14 · Biomedical Equipment Preventive Maintenance Compliance

## Description

Joint Commission requires documented PM programs for medical equipment. Tracking compliance prevents accreditation findings and ensures equipment reliability.

## Value

Joint Commission requires documented PM programs for medical equipment. Tracking compliance prevents accreditation findings and ensures equipment reliability.

## Implementation

Integrate CMMS biomedical work orders. Track PM completion rates by risk level and department. Alert on overdue high-risk equipment. Generate compliance dashboards for accreditation readiness. **Domain context:** Joint Commission EC.02.04.01 and related CMS expectations reference equipment risk stratification and PM completion. **Splunk:** `pm_due_date` comparisons must be epoch-aligned; `now()` uses search-time TZ.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=healthcare sourcetype="cmms:biomed"
| where isnull(pm_completed_date) AND pm_due_date < now()
| eval days_overdue=round((now()-pm_due_date)/86400,0)
| stats count as overdue_count by risk_level
| sort - overdue_count
| table risk_level, overdue_count
```
