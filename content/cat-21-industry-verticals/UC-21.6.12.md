# UC-21.6.12 · Multi-Location Store Infrastructure Comparison

## Description

Benchmarking composite health scores across stores highlights underperforming sites for capital planning and regional support prioritization.

## Value

Benchmarking composite health scores across stores highlights underperforming sites for capital planning and regional support prioritization.

## Implementation

Populate `store:infra` from nightly ETL that rolls up POS, Wi-Fi, and energy KPIs per store. Keep scoring methodology documented for audit. Use for regional scorecards rather than real-time alerting unless scores refresh hourly. **Domain context:** Multi-site benchmarking supports capital allocation (remodel vs close)—ensure `health_score` weighting is agreed with finance and ops. **Splunk:** `eventstats median` across stores on the same `_time` bucket compares peers—watch for missing data skewing medians.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=retail sourcetype="store:infra"
| bin _time span=1d
| stats latest(health_score) as health, avg(pos_latency_ms) as avg_pos, sum(wifi_issue_count) as wifi_issues, latest(energy_kwh_day) as kwh by store_id, _time
| eventstats median(health) as med_health, median(avg_pos) as med_pos by _time
| eval pos_delta=avg_pos-med_pos
| where health < med_health*0.9 OR pos_delta > 200 OR wifi_issues > 15
| sort health
| table _time, store_id, health, med_health, avg_pos, pos_delta, wifi_issues, kwh
```
