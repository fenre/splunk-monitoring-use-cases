# UC-15.2.5 · Water Leak Detection

## Description

Water in a data center causes immediate equipment damage and potential electrical hazards. Seconds matter in detection.

## Value

Water in a data center causes immediate equipment damage and potential electrical hazards. Seconds matter in detection.

## Implementation

Deploy water leak detection sensors under raised floors, near CRAC units, and along pipe routes. Alert at critical priority on any detection. Integrate with building facilities team notification. Test sensors quarterly.
