## Description

Monitors information-security risk-assessment coverage, finding resolution rates, and assessment currency across business functions per GLBA Â§314.4(b) requirements.

## Value

Â§314.4(b) requires financial institutions to conduct risk assessments identifying reasonably foreseeable internal and external risks. Continuous tracking ensures the risk-assessment programme stays current and findings are addressed.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc_compliance sourcetype IN (risk_assessment, vuln_scan_summary) earliest=-90d
| eval risk_identified=if(isnotnull(risk_id) AND risk_rating IN ("critical", "high", "medium", "low"), 1, 0)
| eval risk_mitigated=if(mitigation_status IN ("mitigated", "accepted", "transferred"), 1, 0)
| stats count AS total_risks sum(risk_identified) AS identified sum(risk_mitigated) AS mitigated dc(asset_category) AS asset_categories_assessed latest(assessment_date) AS last_assessment BY business_function
| eval unmitigated=identified - mitigated
| eval mitigation_pct=round(100*mitigated/identified, 1)
| eval days_since_assessment=round((now()-strptime(last_assessment, "%Y-%m-%d"))/86400)
| where unmitigated>0 OR days_since_assessment>365
| table business_function, total_risks, identified, mitigated, unmitigated, mitigation_pct, days_since_assessment, asset_categories_assessed
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest risk-assessment and vulnerability-scan results with risk_id, risk_rating, mitigation_status; (2) schedule quarterly; (3) escalate overdue assessments and unmitigated high risks. Indexes required: `index=grc_compliance`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=risk_assessment`, `sourcetype=vuln_scan_summary`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `asset_categories_assessed`, `business_function`, `days_since_assessment`, `identified`, `last_assessment`, `mitigated`.
