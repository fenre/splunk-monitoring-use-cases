# UC-22.2.41 · NIS2 Management Body Cybersecurity Training Evidence

## Description

Article 20 requires members of management bodies to undergo training and to oversee cyber risk. This use case tracks board and executive training completions, including NIS2-specific modules, and flags gaps before annual general meeting cycles.

## Value

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
| inputlookup nis2_management_roster.csv
| join person_id [
    search index=training course_code="NIS2_EXEC" earliest=-730d
    | stats latest(completion_date) as last_completion by person_id
]
| eval days_since=if(isnotnull(last_completion), round((now()-strptime(last_completion,"%Y-%m-%d"))/86400,0), 9999)
| where days_since > 365 OR isnull(last_completion)
| table person_id, role, last_completion, days_since
```
