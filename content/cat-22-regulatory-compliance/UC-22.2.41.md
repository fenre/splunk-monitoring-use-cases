# 22.2.41 — NIS2 Management Body Cybersecurity Training Evidence

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Article 20 requires members of management bodies to undergo training and to oversee cyber risk. This use case tracks board and executive training completions, including NIS2-specific modules, and flags gaps before annual general meeting cycles.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NIS2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes training. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
| inputlookup nis2_management_roster.csv
| join person_id [
    search index=training course_code="NIS2_EXEC" earliest=-730d
    | stats latest(completion_date) as last_completion by person_id
]
| eval days_since=if(isnotnull(last_completion), round((now()-strptime(last_completion,"%Y-%m-%d"))/86400,0), 9999)
| where days_since > 365 OR isnull(last_completion)
| table person_id, role, last_completion, days_since
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain roster of persons legally considered management body; (2) map LMS course codes; (3) alert governance committee on gaps; (4) store certificates in HR system with Splunk holding only dates; (5) complement UC-22.2.20 governance dashboard. Indexes required: `index=training`; Lookups: `nis2_management_roster`, `nis2_management_roster.csv`.

## Visualization

Table (missing training), Single value (% trained), Bar chart (by role), Timeline (training completions).

## Compliance

- **NIS2** Directive (EU) 2022/2555: Art.20 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing: SPL directly addresses Art.20 requirements with specific, actionable monitoring.

## Known False Positives

Administrative tasks, scheduled jobs or platform updates can match this pattern — correlate with change management, maintenance windows and user role before raising severity.
