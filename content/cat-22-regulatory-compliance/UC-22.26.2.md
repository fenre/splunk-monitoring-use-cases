# UC-22.26.2 · Security clearance tracking

## Description

Joins authentication success events to HR clearance attributes to highlight accounts active after clearance expiry or role change.

## Value

Joins authentication success events to HR clearance attributes to highlight accounts active after clearance expiry or role change.

## Implementation

(1) Maintain supporting lookups (classified hosts, sectors, transfers) aligned to Norwegian regulatory language. (2) Schedule searches with Europe/Oslo time semantics for reporting cutoffs. (3) Route ES notables to the correct national security, OT, or privacy queues. (4) Quarterly false-positive tuning with HR, operations, and legal stakeholders.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=hr_security OR index=identity sourcetype IN ("clearance:hr","okta:user_lifecycle") earliest=-7d
| eval upn=lower(coalesce(user, profile_login))
| stats latest(clearance_level) as clr, latest(_time) as hr_ts by upn
| join type=left upn [| search index=wineventlog sourcetype="WinEventLog:Security" EventCode IN (4624,4634) earliest=-7d | eval upn=lower(user) | stats latest(_time) as last_auth by upn]
| where clr IN ("None","Revoked") AND last_auth>hr_ts
| table upn, clr, hr_ts, last_auth
```
