# UC-22.6.49 · ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 9.1 — Monitoring programme coverage: KPI telemetry uptime

## Description

For every ISMS KPI the committee has agreed to measure under Clause 9.1, check the pipeline is alive within the KPI's freshness SLA. Stale pipelines are a silent failure mode of monitoring programmes.

## Value

Prevents the worst external-audit outcome (monitoring 'was in place' but had silently died) and gives the Head of Platform a single dashboard tile showing ISMS telemetry health.

## Implementation

(1) Push each KPI into index=compliance; (2) maintain iso_27001_kpi_freshness.csv per KPI with its SLA; (3) schedule UC every 15m; (4) is_stale=1 pages the data-engineering owner of the KPI; (5) weekly ISMS report attaches the latest state.

Step 1 — Configure data collection
(1) Push each KPI into index=compliance; (2) maintain iso_27001_kpi_freshness.csv per KPI with its SLA; (3) schedule UC every 15m; (4) is_stale=1 pages the data-engineering owner of the KPI; (5) weekly ISMS report attaches the latest state.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
| inputlookup iso_27001_kpi_freshness.csv
| join type=outer kpi_id
  [search index=compliance sourcetype=isms:objective_result earliest=-7d
   | stats latest(_time) AS last_seen BY kpi_id]
| eval age_min=round((now()-last_seen)/60,0)
| eval is_stale=if(age_min>sla_minutes OR isnull(last_seen),1,0)
| table kpi_id last_seen age_min sla_minutes is_stale
```
