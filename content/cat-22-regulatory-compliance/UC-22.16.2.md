# 22.16.2 — Cross-zone traffic volume spike vs baseline

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "Cross-zone traffic volume spike vs baseline".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ot and sourcetypes pan:traffic. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=ot sourcetype=pan:traffic earliest=-30d dest_port IN (502,20000,44818)
| eval uc_tag="22.16.2"
| bin span=6h _time
| stats sum(bytes) as b by _time, src_ip, dest_ip
| eventstats median(b) as p50 by dest_ip
| where b > p50*4
| table _time, src_ip, dest_ip, b, p50
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t sum(All_Traffic.bytes_in) as agg_value from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest | sort - agg_value
```
