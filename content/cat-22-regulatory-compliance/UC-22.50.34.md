# 22.50.34 — COSO Principle 16 — Ongoing evaluations: continuous monitoring and deficiency-remediation tracking

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Tracks the continuous-monitoring programme's evaluation completeness and deficiency-remediation rates per COSO Principle 16, ensuring ongoing and separate evaluations are performed and findings are resolved.

## Value

COSO Principle 16 requires the entity to select, develop, and perform ongoing and/or separate evaluations. Monitoring programme coverage and deficiency resolution provides auditable evidence of evaluation rigour.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc_compliance sourcetype IN (monitoring_programme, control_deficiency) earliest=-90d
| eval deficiency_remediated=if(status IN ("remediated", "closed"), 1, 0)
| eval evaluation_completed=if(sourcetype="monitoring_programme" AND eval_status="completed", 1, 0)
| stats count AS total_items sum(deficiency_remediated) AS remediated sum(evaluation_completed) AS evaluations_done dc(control_area) AS areas_covered avg(eval(if(deficiency_remediated=1, round((resolution_time - _time)/86400), null()))) AS avg_remediation_days BY programme, quarter
| eval remediation_pct=round(100*remediated/total_items, 1)
| where remediation_pct<100 OR evaluations_done=0
| table programme, quarter, total_items, remediated, remediation_pct, evaluations_done, areas_covered, avg_remediation_days
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest monitoring-programme schedules and control-deficiency events; (2) schedule quarterly; (3) report to audit committee. Indexes required: `index=grc_compliance`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=control_deficiency`, `sourcetype=monitoring_programme`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `areas_covered`, `avg_remediation_days`, `deficiency_remediated`, `evaluation_completed`, `evaluations_done`, `programme`.

## Compliance

- **COSO** 2013 ICFR: Principle16 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Principle16.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
