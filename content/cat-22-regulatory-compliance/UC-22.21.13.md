# 22.21.13 — Autonomous Decision Threshold Breach Monitoring (Art. 14)

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Alerts when decisions are auto-approved above configured risk_score without human gate, evidencing threshold enforcement.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ai_policy and sourcetypes ai:decision:policy, ai:decision:policy`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=ai_policy sourcetype="ai:decision:policy" earliest=-24h auto_approve=true
| lookup ai_risk_thresholds.csv model_id OUTPUT max_auto_risk
| eval breach=if(risk_score > max_auto_risk,1,0)
| where breach=1 OR (auto_approve=true AND human_gate=false AND risk_score>0.6)
| stats count by model_id, user_segment, breach
| sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain thresholds lookup by jurisdiction; (2) correlate with credit/product policies; (3) notable in ES on breach; (4) monthly threshold review attestation. Indexes required: `index=ai_policy`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ai:decision:policy`, `sourcetype=ai:decision:policy``.

## Visualization

## Known False Positives
