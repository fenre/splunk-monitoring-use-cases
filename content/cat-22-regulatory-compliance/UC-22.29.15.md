# 22.29.15 — K-ISMS certification maintenance evidence

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for k-isms certification maintenance evidence aligned with PDPA SG; PIPL; K-ISMS.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SG PDPA compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes kisms:evidence. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype="kisms:evidence" earliest=-365d
| stats latest(status) as st by control_id, system
| where st!="EvidenceUploaded" AND match(control_id,"AC-[0-9]+")
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=kisms:evidence`.
