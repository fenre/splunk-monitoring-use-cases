# 22.50.43 — UN R156 7.1.4 — Software update recording and reporting: update audit trail completeness

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors software update audit trail completeness and regulatory reporting obligations per UN R156 7.1.4, ensuring all updates are properly recorded with RXSWIN identifiers and reported to approval authorities.

## Value

UN R156 requires recording and reporting of all software updates including RXSWIN (Regulation Software Identification Number). Audit trail completeness monitoring ensures regulatory reporting obligations are met.

## SPL

```spl
index=ota_updates sourcetype IN (ota_audit_log, sw_version_registry) earliest=-90d
| eval record_complete=if(isnotnull(rxswin) AND isnotnull(sw_version) AND isnotnull(update_date) AND isnotnull(approval_authority), 1, 0)
| eval reported_to_authority=if(isnotnull(authority_notification_date), 1, 0)
| stats count AS total_updates sum(record_complete) AS complete_records sum(reported_to_authority) AS reported dc(vehicle_type) AS types_covered BY vehicle_type, reporting_period
| eval record_completeness_pct=round(100*complete_records/total_updates, 1)
| eval reporting_pct=round(100*reported/total_updates, 1)
| where record_completeness_pct<100 OR reporting_pct<100
| table vehicle_type, reporting_period, total_updates, complete_records, record_completeness_pct, reported, reporting_pct, types_covered
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest OTA audit logs with rxswin, sw_version, update_date, approval_authority; (2) schedule monthly; (3) flag incomplete records for compliance team. Indexes required: `index=ota_updates`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ota_audit_log`, `sourcetype=sw_version_registry`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `complete_records`, `record_complete`, `record_completeness_pct`, `reported`, `reported_to_authority`, `reporting_pct`.

## Compliance

- **UN R156** 2021: 7.1.4 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for 7.1.4.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
