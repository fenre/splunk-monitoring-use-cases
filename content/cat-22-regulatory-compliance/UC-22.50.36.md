# 22.50.36 — FERPA §99.33 — Redisclosure and record-keeping: downstream-sharing and access-log completeness

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Tracks redisclosure restrictions and record-keeping compliance per FERPA §99.33, which requires institutions to inform recipients that further disclosure is prohibited and to maintain records of each disclosure. Corrects prior GDPR-flavored vocabulary to use FERPA-native terminology.

## Value

§99.33 requires that recipients of education records are notified of redisclosure restrictions and that institutions maintain records of each disclosure. Continuous monitoring ensures these obligations are met.

## SPL

```spl
index=sis sourcetype IN (disclosure_log, redisclosure_tracking) earliest=-30d
| search record_type="education_record" action IN ("disclosure", "redisclosure")
| eval restriction_sent=if(isnotnull(redisclosure_restriction_sent) AND redisclosure_restriction_sent="true", 1, 0)
| eval record_maintained=if(isnotnull(disclosure_record_id) AND disclosure_record_id!="", 1, 0)
| stats count AS total_disclosures sum(restriction_sent) AS with_restriction sum(record_maintained) AS with_record dc(accessor_id) AS distinct_recipients BY system, action
| eval restriction_compliance_pct=if(total_disclosures>0, round(100*with_restriction/total_disclosures, 1), 100)
| eval recordkeeping_pct=if(total_disclosures>0, round(100*with_record/total_disclosures, 1), 100)
| where restriction_compliance_pct < 100 OR recordkeeping_pct < 100
| table system, action, total_disclosures, with_restriction, restriction_compliance_pct, with_record, recordkeeping_pct, distinct_recipients
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest disclosure logs with redisclosure_restriction_sent, accessor_name, legitimate_interest; (2) schedule monthly; (3) flag incomplete records for Registrar remediation. Indexes required: `index=sis_access`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=data_sharing_log`, `sourcetype=education_record_disclosure`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `complete_logs`, `distinct_recipients`, `has_redisclosure_notice`, `log_complete`, `log_completeness_pct`, `notice_pct`.

## Compliance
