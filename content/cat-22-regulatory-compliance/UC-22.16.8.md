# 22.16.8 — Privileged remote maintenance with MFA and recording correlation

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "Privileged remote maintenance with MFA and recording correlation".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes pam, vpn and sourcetypes cyberark:pta, paloalto:globalprotect. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=vpn sourcetype=paloalto:globalprotect portal="pipeline-ot" earliest=-7d
| eval uc_tag="22.16.8"
| stats latest(mfa_factor) as mfa by user, session_id
| join type=left user [
    search index=pam sourcetype=cyberark:pta earliest=-7d | stats latest(recording_id) as rec by user
  ]
| where mfa="none" OR isnull(rec)
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=pam`, `index=vpn`.

## Visualization

Time chart, Choropleth or site bar, Table, Single value. Key fields: `mfa`, `rec`, `session_id`, `uc_tag`, `user`.
