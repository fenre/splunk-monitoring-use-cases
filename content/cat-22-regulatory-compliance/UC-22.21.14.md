# UC-22.21.14 · Human-in-the-Loop Intervention Effectiveness (Art. 14)

## Description

Compares downstream outcomes after override vs baseline auto path to evidence intervention effectiveness for governance reviews.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ai_outcomes and sourcetypes ai:outcome:track. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Align outcome_score definition with business KPI; (2) ensure ethical review sign-off on metrics; (3) quarterly dashboard to board risk committee; (4) document limitations. Indexes required: `index=ai_outcomes`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ai:outcome:track`.

Step 1 — Configure data collection
(1) Align outcome_score definition with business KPI; (2) ensure ethical review sign-off on metrics; (3) quarterly dashboard to board risk committee; (4) document limitations.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=ai_outcomes sourcetype="ai:outcome:track" earliest=-90d
| stats median(outcome_score) as med_score, median(days_to_outcome) as med_days by path, product_line
| eval uplift=if(path="override", med_score, null())
| eventstats max(eval(if(path="auto", med_score, null()))) as base by product_line
| eval delta=if(path="override", med_score-base, null())
| where path="override"
| table product_line, med_score, base, delta, med_days
```
