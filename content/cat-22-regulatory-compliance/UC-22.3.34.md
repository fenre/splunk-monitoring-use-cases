# UC-22.3.34 · DORA Outsourcing Registers — Data Localization and Cross-Border Transfer Field Validation

## Description

Outsourcing often imposes data residency constraints. Validating that register rows specify processing locations and transfer mechanisms reduces the risk of unlawful transfers and incomplete RoI disclosures.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for DORA compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Legal maintains `restricted_data_categories.csv`; (2) do not store SCC text in Splunk—boolean flags only; (3) alert privacy office; (4) sync with DPIA references; (5) annual RoI sign-off pack.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
| inputlookup dora_outsourcing_register.csv
| where isnull(processing_regions) OR processing_regions="" OR isnull(transfer_mechanism) OR transfer_mechanism=""
| lookup restricted_data_categories.csv data_categories OUTPUT requires_scc
| where requires_scc="true" AND transfer_mechanism!="SCC"
| table contract_id, processing_regions, transfer_mechanism, data_categories
```
