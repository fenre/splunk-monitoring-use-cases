# 22.30.22 — Incident notification within 72 hours tracking

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports APRA CPS 234 supervisory examination readiness for incident notification within 72 hours tracking.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APRA CPS 234 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes cps234:incident. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype="cps234:incident" earliest=-30d
| eval hrs=(now()-strptime(detected_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S"))/3600
| where isnull(board_notified_at) AND hrs>12
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Authentication.Authentication by Authentication.action, Authentication.user, Authentication.src | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_material_services.csv` for important business services and regulator tags. (2) Send weekly digests to CRO and technology risk committees. (3) Validate DB Connect concurrency for audit peak windows. (4) Archive results to a restricted summary index for retention governance. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cps234:incident`.
