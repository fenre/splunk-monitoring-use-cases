# 22.13.7 — Delegated Authority Register Change History

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We retain an auditable history when delegated authority for cyber security duties is granted or revoked, supporting management control evidence.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes cip:delegation_register, cip:delegation_register`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype=cip:delegation_register earliest=-730d
| eval eff=strptime(effective_date,"%Y-%m-%d")
| eval rev=if(isnotnull(revoked_date),strptime(revoked_date,"%Y-%m-%d"),null())
| eval active=if(isnull(rev) OR rev>now(),1,0)
| stats latest(scope) as scope latest(effective_date) as effective latest(revoked_date) as revoked by delegate
| eval status=if(isnull(revoked) OR revoked="","active","revoked")
| sort delegate
```

## Implementation

(1) Push delegation CSV rows append-only through a scripted input or HEC. (2) Align fields with the official register. (3) Quarterly PDF export for legal retention. (4) Correlate revocations with HR events. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cip:delegation_register`, `sourcetype=cip:delegation_register``.
