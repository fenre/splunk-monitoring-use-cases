# 22.22.11 — Cross-Border Payment Anomaly Detection (Corridor Concentration)

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports PSD2 security objectives and EBA fraud monitoring expectations by correlating payment, authentication, and fraud-engine telemetry for typology coverage.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes swift and sourcetypes cbpr:mt103. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=swift sourcetype="cbpr:mt103" earliest=-24h
| eval psd2_fraud_tag="xb"
| eval high_value=if(amount_eur>5000 OR amount>5000,1,0)
| stats count as events, dc(user_id) as users, sum(high_value) as hv, median(eval(if(isnum(score),score,null()))) as med_score by psd2_fraud_tag, bin(_time,15m)
| eval uc_id="22.22.11"
| where events > 75
| sort - events
| head 200
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=swift`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cbpr:mt103`.
