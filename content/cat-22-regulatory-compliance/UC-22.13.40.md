# 22.13.40 — Incident Response Timeline Milestone Tracking

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We track detection, containment, eradication, and recovery timestamps from ITSM to evidence structured incident response progression.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsm and sourcetypes incident:milestone, incident:milestone`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype=incident:milestone category="CIP008" earliest=-90d
| eval det=strptime(detected_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval con=strptime(contained_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval rec=strptime(recovered_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval contain_h=round((con-det)/3600,2)
| eval recover_h=round((rec-con)/3600,2)
| table incident_id, detected_at, contained_at, recovered_at, contain_h, recover_h
```

## Implementation

(1) Require milestone fields on CIP-tagged incidents. (2) Validate timestamps in UTC. (3) Dashboard for leadership. (4) Export for after-action reviews. Indexes required: `index=itsm`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=incident:milestone`, `sourcetype=incident:milestone``.

## Visualization
