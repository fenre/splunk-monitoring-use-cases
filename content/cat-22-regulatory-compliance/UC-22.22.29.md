# 22.22.29 — Fraud Loss Reporting Aggregation by Product Line

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports incident governance, customer communications evidence, and supervisory reporting workflows associated with payment service continuity and fraud statistics.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes payments and sourcetypes pay:fraud:loss. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=payments sourcetype="pay:fraud:loss" earliest=-400d
| eval inc_tag="loss"
| stats sum(loss_eur) as total_loss, dc(case_id) as cases by product_line, reporting_quarter
| eventstats median(total_loss) as med_loss by reporting_quarter
| eval uc_id="22.22.29"
| eval uplift=round(total_loss-med_loss,2)
| where uplift>10000 OR total_loss>250000
| sort - total_loss
| table uc_id, reporting_quarter, product_line, cases, total_loss, med_loss, uplift
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=payments`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=pay:fraud:loss`.

## Visualization

Timeline (milestones), Table (late NCA notifications), Single value (mismatches). Key fields: `cases`, `inc_tag`, `med_loss`, `product_line`, `reporting_quarter`, `total_loss`.

## Compliance

- **PSD2** Directive (EU) 2015/2366: Art.96 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Art.96.

## Known False Positives

Administrative tasks, scheduled jobs or platform updates can match this pattern — correlate with change management, maintenance windows and user role before raising severity.
