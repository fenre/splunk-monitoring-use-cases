# 22.13.16 — ESP Outbound Bytes Spike versus Rolling Baseline

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We surface unusual outbound volume from ESP-internal segments that may indicate data exfiltration or misconfigured services.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes network and sourcetypes pan:traffic, pan:traffic`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=network sourcetype=pan:traffic src_zone="ESP_INTERNAL" action=allowed earliest=-30d
| eval internal_dest=cidrmatch("10.0.0.0/8",dest) OR cidrmatch("172.16.0.0/12",dest) OR cidrmatch("192.168.0.0/16",dest)
| where internal_dest=0
| bin _time span=1d
| stats sum(bytes_out) as b_out by src, dest, _time
| eventstats median(b_out) as med by src, dest
| eval spike=if(b_out>med*8 AND med>0,1,0)
| where spike=1
| table _time, src, dest, b_out, med
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t sum(All_Traffic.bytes_out) as agg_value from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest span=1d | sort - agg_value
```

## Implementation
