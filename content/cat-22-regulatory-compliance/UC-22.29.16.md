# 22.29.16 — Technical measures adequacy review

**Criticality:** low | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for technical measures adequacy review aligned with PDPA SG; PIPL; K-ISMS.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SG PDPA compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes edr and sourcetypes DefenderATP:Host, crowdstrike:hosts. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=edr sourcetype="crowdstrike:hosts" OR sourcetype="DefenderATP:Host" earliest=-24h
| stats latest(EncryptionStatus) as enc by hostname, os_version
| where enc!="Encrypted"
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=edr`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=DefenderATP:Host`, `sourcetype=crowdstrike:hosts`.
