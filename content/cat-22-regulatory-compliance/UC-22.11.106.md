# 22.11.106 — PCI-DSS 12.3 — Targeted risk analysis: frequency adherence for per-requirement TRAs

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

PCI-DSS v4.0 introduced targeted risk analyses (TRAs) at 12.3. This UC makes the refresh cadence measurable.

## Value

Gives compliance an explicit overdue list — the v4.0 TRA is a common reason for 'in progress' at year-two assessments.

## SPL

```spl
| inputlookup pci_tra_register.csv
| join type=outer tra_id
  [search index=grc sourcetype=archer:tra_record earliest=-2y
   | stats latest(_time) AS last_refresh_at BY tra_id]
| eval overdue_days=round((now()-last_refresh_at)/86400,0)-frequency_days
| where overdue_days>0 OR isnull(last_refresh_at)
| table tra_id requirement frequency_days last_refresh_at overdue_days
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain pci_tra_register.csv with requirement and frequency_days; (2) GRC writes each refresh event; (3) schedule UC daily; (4) overdue TRAs open a CISO-owned task; (5) quarterly compliance report. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=archer:tra_record`, `sourcetype=archer:tra_record;`.

## Visualization

Table of overdue TRAs, bar chart by requirement, single value 'TRAs overdue'.

## Compliance

- **PCI-DSS** v4.0: 12.3 (full) — PCI-DSS 12.3 Targeted risk analysis — cadence adherence is direct evidence.
- **ISO/IEC 27001** 2022: 8.2 (partial) — ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 8.2 Information security risk assessment — contributing.
- **DORA** Regulation (EU) 2022/2554: Art.6 (full) — Uplifted to full: SPL provides comprehensive, standalone evidence for Art.6 with specific data sources and actionable thresholds.

## Known False Positives

TRAs whose requirement is marked 'not applicable' should be excluded via scope_applicable=false.
