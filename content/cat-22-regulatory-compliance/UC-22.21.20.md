# 22.21.20 — Risk Management System — Hazard Log Update SLA (Art. 9/Annex IV)

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Measures time from new_hazard to hazard_reviewed in risk register stream for RMS operational effectiveness.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ai_risk and sourcetypes rms:hazard, rms:hazard`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=ai_risk sourcetype="rms:hazard" earliest=-90d
| eval opened=if(status="new",_time,null())
| eval reviewed=if(status="reviewed",_time,null())
| stats min(opened) as t_open, max(reviewed) as t_rev by hazard_id
| eval review_hours=round((t_rev-t_open)/3600,2)
| where isnull(t_rev) OR review_hours>168
| sort - review_hours
```

## Implementation

(1) Ensure hazard_id stable; (2) escalate unreviewed >7d; (3) link to incident index; (4) quarterly RMS committee export. Indexes required: `index=ai_risk`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=rms:hazard`, `sourcetype=rms:hazard``.
