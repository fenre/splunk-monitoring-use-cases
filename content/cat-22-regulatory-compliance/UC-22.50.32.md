# 22.50.32 — COSO Principle 5 — Accountability enforcement: segregation-of-duties and access-review evidence

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors segregation-of-duties violations and access-review completion rates to provide evidence that the organisation enforces accountability per COSO Principle 5.

## Value

COSO Principle 5 requires that the organisation holds individuals accountable for internal control responsibilities. SoD monitoring and access-review tracking provides auditable evidence of accountability enforcement.

## SPL

```spl
index=iam sourcetype IN (role_assignments, sod_violations, access_reviews) earliest=-30d
| eval is_sod_conflict=if(sourcetype="sod_violations", 1, 0)
| eval review_completed=if(sourcetype="access_reviews" AND review_status="completed", 1, 0)
| stats sum(is_sod_conflict) AS sod_violations dc(eval(if(sourcetype="access_reviews", campaign_id, null()))) AS review_campaigns sum(review_completed) AS reviews_completed dc(user) AS users_reviewed BY business_unit
| eval sod_risk=if(sod_violations>0, "elevated", "normal")
| where sod_violations>0 OR reviews_completed=0
| table business_unit, sod_violations, sod_risk, review_campaigns, reviews_completed, users_reviewed
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest IAM role-assignment and SoD-violation events; (2) ingest access-review campaign results; (3) schedule weekly; (4) escalate SoD conflicts to compliance team. Indexes required: `index=iam`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=access_reviews`, `sourcetype=role_assignments`, `sourcetype=sod_violations`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `business_unit`, `is_sod_conflict`, `review_campaigns`, `review_completed`, `reviews_completed`, `sod_risk`.

## Compliance

- **COSO** 2013 ICFR: Principle5 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Principle5.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
