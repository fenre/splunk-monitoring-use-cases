# UC-22.26.14 · HSE system audit trails

## Description

Tracks privileged edits to occupational health and safety systems tied to offshore operations.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NO Petroleumsforskriften compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes app and sourcetypes hse:audit, hse:incident. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Maintain supporting lookups (classified hosts, sectors, transfers) aligned to Norwegian regulatory language. (2) Schedule searches with Europe/Oslo time semantics for reporting cutoffs. (3) Route ES notables to the correct national security, OT, or privacy queues. (4) Quarterly false-positive tuning with HR, operations, and legal stakeholders. Indexes required: `index=app`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=hse:audit`, `sourcetype=hse:incident`.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=app sourcetype="hse:incident" earliest=-180d status="closed"
| join type=inner incident_id [| search index=app sourcetype="hse:audit" action="update" | stats max(_time) as audit_t by incident_id]
| eval closed_epoch=strptime(closed_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| where audit_t > closed_epoch
| table incident_id, closed_at, audit_t, actor
```
