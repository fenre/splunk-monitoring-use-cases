# 22.21.5 — Bias Drift Monitoring Across Protected Attributes (Art. 12)

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Tracks disparate impact ratio across demographic buckets over sliding windows to evidence ongoing bias monitoring aligned with Art. 12 and Annex IV risk management.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ai_fairness and sourcetypes ai:bias:eval, ai:bias:eval`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=ai_fairness sourcetype="ai:bias:eval" earliest=-48h
| stats latest(selection_rate) as sr by attribute, bucket, model_id
| eventstats max(sr) as sr_max, min(sr) as sr_min by attribute, model_id
| eval dipr=round(sr_max/nullif(sr_min,0),3)
| where dipr > 1.25 OR sr_min < 0.01
| sort attribute, -dipr
| table model_id, attribute, bucket, sr, dipr
```

## Implementation

(1) Ensure fairness jobs emit non-identifying buckets; (2) join reference rates via DB Connect if needed; (3) alert when DIPR exceeds policy; (4) store model card link in lookup. Indexes required: `index=ai_fairness`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ai:bias:eval`, `sourcetype=ai:bias:eval``.

## Visualization
