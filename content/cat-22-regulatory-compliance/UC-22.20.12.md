# 22.20.12 — CMMC Level 3 enhanced practice — threat scenario 2

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Demonstrates contractor cybersecurity hygiene for "CMMC Level 3 enhanced practice — threat scenario 2" using CUI-scoped telemetry mapped to CMMC 2.0 Level 3 expectations.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for CMMC compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes notable. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=notable earliest=-7d
| eval uc_tag="22.20.12"
| where match(rule_name,"(?i)CUI|CMMC") AND status IN ("New","In Progress")
| stats count by urgency, owner
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Endpoint.Processes by Processes.process_name, Processes.user, Processes.dest | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=notable`.

## Visualization

Stacked bar, Table, Time chart, Single value. Key fields: `owner`, `uc_tag`, `urgency`.

## Compliance

- **CMMC** 2.0: SI.L2-3.14.6 (contributing) — ES notable correlation for CUI-scoped threat scenarios supports attack monitoring.

## Known False Positives
