# UC-22.26.5 · Information system accreditation evidence

## Description

Aggregates control tests, exceptions, and approvals into a quarterly accreditation evidence view.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NO Sikkerhetsloven compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes change and sourcetypes servicenow:change, snow:chg. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Maintain supporting lookups (classified hosts, sectors, transfers) aligned to Norwegian regulatory language. (2) Schedule searches with Europe/Oslo time semantics for reporting cutoffs. (3) Route ES notables to the correct national security, OT, or privacy queues. (4) Quarterly false-positive tuning with HR, operations, and legal stakeholders. Indexes required: `index=change`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=servicenow:change`, `sourcetype=snow:chg`.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=change sourcetype IN ("snow:chg","servicenow:change") earliest=-30d (short_description="*accred*" OR cmdb_ci="*CL-*")
| stats values(state) as states, min(_time) as opened, max(_time) as updated by number, cmdb_ci
| eval reopened=if(mvcount(mvfilter(match(states,"(Implement|Reopened)"))) > 0, 1, 0)
| table number, cmdb_ci, opened, updated, reopened
| sort - updated
```
