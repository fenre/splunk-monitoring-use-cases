# 22.21.25 — Regulatory Authority Notification Delivery Confirmation (Art. 73)

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Confirms secure notification channel message_id acknowledged by authority mailbox/API within policy window.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes mail and sourcetypes o365:messaging, o365:messaging`, sendgrid:event. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=mail (sourcetype="o365:messaging" OR sourcetype="sendgrid:event") earliest=-30d recipient_domain="*authority*"
| stats earliest(eval(if(event="queued",_time,null()))) as t_q,
        earliest(eval(if(event="delivered",_time,null()))) as t_d,
        earliest(eval(if(match(event,"bounce|dropped"),_time,null()))) as t_fail
    by message_id
| eval deliver_h=round((t_d-t_q)/3600,3)
| where isnull(t_d) OR isnotnull(t_fail)
| table message_id, t_q, t_d, t_fail, deliver_h
```

## Implementation

(1) Tag regulatory messages with dedicated header; (2) alert on bounce; (3) retain DKIM pass evidence; (4) legal owns recipient allowlist. Indexes required: `index=mail`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=o365:messaging`, `sourcetype=o365:messaging``, `sourcetype=sendgrid:event`, `sourcetype=sendgrid:event``.

## Visualization

Table (failed deliveries), Time chart (deliver_h), Single value (undelivered count)
