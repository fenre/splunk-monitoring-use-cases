# 22.29.24 — Annual privacy program assessment evidence

**Criticality:** low | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for annual privacy program assessment evidence aligned with APPI; PDPA Thailand.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APPI compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes audit and sourcetypes privacy:annual_review. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=audit sourcetype="privacy:annual_review" earliest=-400d
| stats latest(score) as s by org_unit, year
| where s<85
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=audit`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=privacy:annual_review`.
