# 22.13.41 — NERC Filing Deadline Compliance Countdown

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We compute hours remaining until regulatory filing deadlines from the official incident discovery time to reduce missed submissions.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsm and sourcetypes cip008_incident, cip008_incident`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype=cip008_incident earliest=-60d
| eval disc=strptime(discovered_at,"%Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%SZ")
| eval due=strptime(filing_deadline,"%Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%SZ")
| eval hours_left=round((due-now())/3600,2)
| eval filed=if(isnotnull(filed_at),1,0)
| where filed=0
| table incident_id, discovered_at, filing_deadline, hours_left, owner
| sort hours_left
```

## Implementation

(1) Populate deadlines per NERC filing category from legal. (2) Alert at seventy-two, twenty-four, and six hours. (3) Attach filing confirmation artifact. (4) Restrict access to sensitive fields. Indexes required: `index=itsm`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cip008_incident`, `sourcetype=cip008_incident``.

## Visualization
