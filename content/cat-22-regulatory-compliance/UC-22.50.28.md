# 22.50.28 — COBIT MEA02.01 — Internal-control monitoring: control-exception tracking and remediation evidence

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Tracks internal control exceptions and audit findings aging against remediation SLAs per COBIT MEA02.01. Uses the explicit opened_at field for age calculation rather than event ingestion time, providing accurate overdue metrics.

## Value

MEA02.01 requires continuous monitoring of the internal-control system. Tracking exceptions and remediation timelines provides evidence that the control environment is actively managed.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc_compliance sourcetype IN (control_exceptions, audit_findings) earliest=-90d
| eval opened_date=strptime(coalesce(opened_at, strftime(_time, "%Y-%m-%d")), "%Y-%m-%d")
| eval days_open=round((now()-opened_date)/86400)
| eval overdue=if(days_open > remediation_sla_days, 1, 0)
| stats count AS total_exceptions sum(overdue) AS overdue_count avg(days_open) AS avg_age max(days_open) AS max_age dc(control_id) AS distinct_controls BY control_domain, severity
| eval overdue_pct=round(100*overdue_count/total_exceptions, 1)
| where overdue_count>0
| sort -overdue_count
| table control_domain, severity, total_exceptions, overdue_count, overdue_pct, avg_age, max_age, distinct_controls
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest control-exception and audit-finding data with control_id, control_domain, severity, and remediation_sla_days; (2) schedule weekly; (3) escalate overdue items to Internal Audit dashboard. Indexes required: `index=grc_compliance`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=audit_findings`, `sourcetype=control_exceptions`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `avg_age`, `control_domain`, `days_open`, `distinct_controls`, `max_age`, `overdue`.

## Compliance

- **COBIT** 2019: MEA02.01 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for MEA02.01.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
