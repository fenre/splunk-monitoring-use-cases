# 22.10.31 — Facility vs Logical Access — Badge-In Without VPN/SSO for Remote Roles

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** verified

## Description

Correlates physical badge events with absence of corresponding VPN or SSO session for users tagged as onsite-only, highlighting impossible travel or credential sharing.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for HIPAA Security compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes pacs, physical, vpn and sourcetypes genetec:access, genetec:access`, paloalto:globalprotect. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=physical sourcetype="genetec:access" result="Granted" earliest=-1d
| lookup badge_to_user.csv badge_id OUTPUT samaccountname, work_mode
| where work_mode="OnsiteOnly"
| eval day=strftime(_time,"%Y-%m-%d")
| join type=left samaccountname, day [
    search index=vpn sourcetype="paloalto:globalprotect" earliest=-1d
    | eval day=strftime(_time,"%Y-%m-%d")
    | stats earliest(_time) as vpn_first by src_user, day
    | rename src_user as samaccountname ]
| where isnull(vpn_first)
| stats count by samaccountname, reader, day
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize time zones for multi-campus; (2) tune for vendor remote workers; (3) integrate HR work location attribute. Indexes required: `index=pacs`, `index=physical`, `index=vpn`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=genetec:access`, `sourcetype=genetec:access``, `sourcetype=paloalto:globalprotect`; Lookups: `badge_to_user`, `badge_to_user.csv`.
