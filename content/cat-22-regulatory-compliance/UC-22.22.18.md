# 22.22.18 — Mutual TLS and OAuth Client Certificate Authentication Audits

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Monitors third-party provider access patterns, consent alignment, and API security controls required for PSD2 open banking operational resilience.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes openbanking and sourcetypes ob:api:access. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=openbanking sourcetype="ob:api:access" earliest=-24h
| eval api_tag="mtls"
| eval is_prod=if(match(environment,"(?i)prod"),1,0)
| eval scope_mismatch=if(isnotnull(consent_scope) AND isnotnull(accessed_scope) AND consent_scope!=accessed_scope,1,0)
| stats count as calls, sum(eval(http_status>=400)) as err, sum(scope_mismatch) as sm, dc(tpp_id) as tpp by aspsp_id, api_tag, is_prod
| eval uc_id="22.22.18"
| eval err_rate=round(100*err/calls,2)
| where err_rate>2 OR sm>0 OR (api_tag="env" AND is_prod=0 AND tpp>50)
| sort - err_rate
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Web.Web by Web.status, Web.http_method, Web.dest | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=openbanking`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ob:api:access`.
