# 22.29.21 — Privacy impact assessment tracking

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for privacy impact assessment tracking aligned with APPI; PDPA Thailand.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APPI compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes pia:workflow. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype="pia:workflow" earliest=-365d
| stats min(_time) as opened, max(_time) as updated by pia_id
| eval duration_days=round((updated-opened)/86400,1)
| where duration_days>90
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=pia:workflow`.

## Visualization

Stacked bar (jurisdiction x action), Table (open reviews), Single value (exports without mechanism). Key fields: `duration_days`, `opened`, `pia_id`, `updated`.
