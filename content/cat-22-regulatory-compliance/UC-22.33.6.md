# 22.33.6 — SAMA cybersecurity framework control testing

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Aligns SAMA Cyber Security Framework supervisory expectations with measurable Splunk evidence for sama cybersecurity framework control testing.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SAMA CSF compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes bank and sourcetypes sama:csf:event. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=bank sourcetype="sama:csf:event" earliest=-30d
| eval reported=strptime(reported_sama_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval detected=strptime(detected_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| where isnull(reported) OR (reported-detected)>4*3600
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize bilingual field extractions in props.conf where required. (2) Map IP ranges and business units to national critical infrastructure and banking registers. (3) Route SAMA, QCB, NESA, and PDPL alerts to regional bilingual runbooks. (4) Weekly leadership digest with regulator-specific views. Indexes required: `index=bank`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=sama:csf:event`.
