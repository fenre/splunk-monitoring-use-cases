# 22.16.18 — Post-incident recovery test evidence in problem records

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "Post-incident recovery test evidence in problem records".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsm and sourcetypes snow:problem. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype=snow:problem category="OT Cyber Incident" earliest=-365d
| eval uc_tag="22.16.18"
| eval ok=if(match(work_notes,"(?i)restored") AND match(work_notes,"(?i)tested"),1,0)
| where ok=0 AND state="Closed"
| table number, short_description, assignment_group
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=itsm`.
