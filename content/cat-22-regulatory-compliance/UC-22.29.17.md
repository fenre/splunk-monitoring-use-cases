# 22.29.17 — Encryption and pseudonymization control evidence

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for encryption and pseudonymization control evidence aligned with PDPA SG; PIPL; K-ISMS.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SG PDPA compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes kms and sourcetypes vault:audit. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=kms sourcetype="vault:audit" earliest=-7d
| stats count by key_path, operation
| where operation="decrypt" AND match(key_path,"(?i)pii")
| sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=kms`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=vault:audit`.
