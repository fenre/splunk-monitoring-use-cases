# UC-22.12.16 · General ledger database backup success within policy windows

## Description

Supports SOX IT general and application controls evidence for "General ledger database backup success within policy windows" by correlating security, change, ERP, and ITSM telemetry with control ownership records, strengthening SOX ITGC attestation.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SOX ITGC compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes backup and sourcetypes commvault:job. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=backup`.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=backup sourcetype=commvault:job earliest=-14d subclient_name="*GLDB*"
| eval uc_tag="22.12.16"
| eval day=strftime(_time,"%Y-%m-%d")
| stats latest(status) as last_status by subclient_name, day
| eval ok=if(match(last_status,"(?i)success|completed"),1,0)
| timechart span=1d sum(ok) by subclient_name
```
