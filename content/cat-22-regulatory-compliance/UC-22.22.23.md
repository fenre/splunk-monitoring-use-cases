# 22.22.23 — Dormant Account Activity After Long Inactivity

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides transaction-level integrity and monitoring evidence aligned with PSD2 operational and security expectations for ASPSPs and payment processors.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes payments and sourcetypes core:account:activity. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=payments sourcetype="core:account:activity" earliest=-24h
| eval txn_tag="dormant"
| eval eur_equiv=coalesce(amount_eur, amount*fx_rate)
| stats count as txns, dc(authorization_id) as auth_dc, sum(eval(eur_equiv>100000)) as very_high by merchant_id, txn_tag, bin(_time,1h)
| eval dup_suspect=if(txns>auth_dc AND txn_tag="dup", txns-auth_dc, 0)
| eval uc_id="22.22.23"
| where very_high>0 OR dup_suspect>5 OR txn_tag="refund"
| sort - very_high
| head 200
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=payments`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=core:account:activity`.
