# UC-22.10.40 · Research Access — Chart Views Without Active IRB Consent Flag

## Description

Correlates research coordinator EHR access with IRB-approved patient rosters to catch out-of-protocol chart reviews.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for HIPAA Security compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes epic and sourcetypes epic:audit, epic:audit`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Standardize study context logging in Hyperspace build; (2) IRB office owns roster refresh; (3) integrate with Office for Human Research Protections policies. Indexes required: `index=epic`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=epic:audit`, `sourcetype=epic:audit``; Lookups: `irb_active_subjects`, `irb_active_subjects.csv`.

• Ensure the following data sources are available: `index=epic` `sourcetype=epic:audit`, `lookup irb_active_subjects.csv` (`STUDY_ID`, `PAT_ID`, `consent_status`, `window_end`).
• For app installation, inputs.conf, and Splunk directory layout, see the Implementation guide: docs/implementation-guide.md

Step 1 — Configure data collection
(1) Standardize study context logging in Hyperspace build; (2) IRB office owns roster refresh; (3) integrate with Office for Human Research Protections policies.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=epic sourcetype="epic:audit" Activity="*Research*" OR Context="*Study*" earliest=-30d
| rex field=Context "STUDY:(?<STUDY_ID>[^|]+)"
| lookup irb_active_subjects.csv STUDY_ID PAT_ID OUTPUT consent_status, window_end
| eval in_window=if(_time<strptime(window_end,"%Y-%m-%d"),1,0)
| where consent_status!="Active" OR in_window=0 OR isnull(consent_status)
| stats values(STUDY_ID) as studies dc(PAT_ID) as patients by USER_ID
| sort - patients
```
