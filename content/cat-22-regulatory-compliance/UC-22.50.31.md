# 22.50.31 — COSO Principle 1 — Commitment to integrity: ethics-hotline and code-of-conduct violation monitoring

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors ethics and conduct reporting effectiveness as evidence for COSO Principle 1 (commitment to integrity and ethical values). Adds exception-based filtering to surface only cases where open reports exist, resolution rates fall below 90%, or average resolution exceeds 30 days.

## Value

COSO Principle 1 requires the organisation to demonstrate commitment to integrity and ethical values. Continuous monitoring of ethics reports and their resolution provides auditable evidence of tone-at-the-top.

## SPL

```spl
index=hr_compliance sourcetype IN (ethics_hotline, conduct_violations) earliest=-90d
| eval resolved=if(status IN ("closed", "resolved", "remediated"), 1, 0)
| eval days_to_resolve=if(resolved=1, round((resolution_time - _time)/86400), round((now() - _time)/86400))
| stats count AS total_reports sum(resolved) AS resolved_count avg(days_to_resolve) AS avg_resolution_days dc(reporter_department) AS departments_reporting values(category) AS categories BY severity
| eval open_count=total_reports - resolved_count
| eval resolution_pct=round(100*resolved_count/total_reports, 1)
| where open_count > 0 OR resolution_pct < 90 OR avg_resolution_days > 30
| table severity, total_reports, resolved_count, open_count, resolution_pct, avg_resolution_days, departments_reporting, categories
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest ethics-hotline and conduct-violation events with category, severity, status, resolution_time; (2) schedule monthly; (3) trend analysis for board reporting. Indexes required: `index=hr_compliance`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=conduct_violations`, `sourcetype=ethics_hotline`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `avg_resolution_days`, `categories`, `days_to_resolve`, `departments_reporting`, `open_count`, `resolution_pct`.

## Compliance

- **COSO** 2013 ICFR: Principle1 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Principle1.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
