Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype=snow:incident category="Pipeline Cyber" earliest=-365d
| eval uc_tag="22.18.29"

| eval resolved=strptime(resolved_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval mttr_h=round((resolved-opened)/3600,2)
| stats median(mttr_h) as p50_mttr by assignment_group
```
