# 22.38.2 — SCC / adequacy decision reference freshness — stale-safeguard detector

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** verified

## Description

Cross-references the transfer register against the official SCC templates and the Art.45 adequacy register. Entries that rely on stale or rescinded safeguards appear immediately.

## Value

Prevents silent expiration of the legal basis for international transfers — the single most common finding in SCC-era enforcement.

## SPL

```spl
| inputlookup transfer_register.csv
| lookup scc_reference.csv template_id OUTPUT template_active effective_from superseded_by
| eval age_days=round((now()-strptime(effective_from,"%Y-%m-%d"))/86400,0)
| where NOT template_active OR age_days>1095 OR isnotnull(superseded_by)
| table transfer_id destination template_id template_active age_days superseded_by
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain scc_reference.csv synced nightly against EDPB / Commission publications; (2) maintain transfer_register.csv as part of Art.30 ROPA; (3) run daily.

## Visualization

Stacked bar of active vs stale vs rescinded, table of findings, single-value '% of transfers with fresh safeguards'. Key fields: `age_days`, `destination`, `superseded_by`, `template_active`, `template_id`, `transfer_id`.

## Compliance

- **GDPR** 2016/679: Art.46 (full) — Art.46 requires appropriate safeguards; out-of-date SCCs (e.g. pre-2021 Module mismatches) are no longer appropriate and must be re-papered.
- **GDPR** 2016/679: Art.45 (partial) — Art.45 adequacy decisions — the UC flags transfers that rely on a superseded or rescinded decision (e.g. Privacy Shield).
- **UK GDPR** post-Brexit: Art.46 (partial) — Derived from GDPR@2016/679 Art.46 (parent assurance='full'). UK GDPR preserves parent clause numbering; inherited assurance degraded one step.
- **UK GDPR** post-Brexit: Art.45 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Art.45.

## Known False Positives

Transfer entries mid-migration (with `migration=in_flight`) should be allow-listed by a start/end date.
