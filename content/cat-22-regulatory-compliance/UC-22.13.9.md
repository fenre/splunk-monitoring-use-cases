# 22.13.9 — Security Awareness Training Completion for BES Personnel

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We list personnel with active BES access who have not completed the current awareness module so training evidence is current before audits.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes hr and sourcetypes lms:completion, lms:completion`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
| inputlookup bes_privileged_users.csv
| join type=left employee_id [
    search index=hr sourcetype=lms:completion course_id="CIP_SECURITY_AWARENESS" earliest=-400d
    | stats latest(completed_at) as completed_at by employee_id
  ]
| eval completed_epoch=strptime(completed_at,"%Y-%m-%d")
| eval stale=if(isnull(completed_epoch) OR completed_epoch<relative_time(now(),"-350d@d"),1,0)
| where stale=1
| table employee_id, role, completed_at
```

## Implementation

(1) Sync LMS completions nightly. (2) Align `employee_id` with directory accounts. (3) Alert managers fourteen days before the annual due wave. (4) Export CSV for the training coordinator. Indexes required: `index=hr`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=lms:completion`, `sourcetype=lms:completion``; Lookups: `bes_privileged_users`, `bes_privileged_users.csv`.

## Visualization
