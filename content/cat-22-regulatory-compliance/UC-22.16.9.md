# 22.16.9 — Contractor MFA gaps to OT bastion and VPN portals

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "Contractor MFA gaps to OT bastion and VPN portals".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes o365 and sourcetypes ms:aad:signin. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=o365 sourcetype=ms:aad:signin earliest=-24h
| eval uc_tag="22.16.9"
| where appDisplayName="Pipeline OT Bastion" AND authenticationRequirement!="multiFactorAuthentication" AND resultType="Success"
| stats count by userPrincipalName, ipAddress
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```
