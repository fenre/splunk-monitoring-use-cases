# 22.16.13 — OT alert volume by NIST CSF-style category

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "OT alert volume by NIST CSF-style category".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ot and sourcetypes nozomi:alert. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=ot sourcetype=nozomi:alert earliest=-7d severity>="high"
| eval uc_tag="22.16.13"
| eval nist_cat=case(match(name,"(?i)malware"),"DE.CM", match(name,"(?i)lateral"),"DE.AE", 1=1,"PR.DS")
| stats count by nist_cat, site_name
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=ot`.

## Visualization

Time chart, Choropleth or site bar, Table, Single value. Key fields: `nist_cat`, `site_name`, `uc_tag`.
