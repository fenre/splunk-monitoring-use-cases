# UC-22.22.30 · Quarterly Statistical Reporting Dataset Reconciliation

## Description

Supports incident governance, customer communications evidence, and supervisory reporting workflows associated with payment service continuity and fraud statistics.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes payments and sourcetypes pay:reg:stats. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=payments`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=pay:reg:stats`.

## Detailed Implementation

• For app installation, inputs.conf, and Splunk directory layout, see the Implementation guide: docs/implementation-guide.md

Step 1 — Configure data collection
(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=payments sourcetype="pay:reg:stats" earliest=-400d
| eval inc_tag="stats"
| stats sum(eval(mismatch="true")) as mismatches, count as rows by aspsp_id, reporting_quarter
| eval uc_id="22.22.30"
| eval mismatch_rate=round(100*mismatches/rows,2)
| where mismatches>0 OR mismatch_rate>0.5
| sort - mismatches
| table uc_id, aspsp_id, reporting_quarter, mismatches, rows, mismatch_rate
```
