# 22.29.20 — DPIA completion for high-risk processing

**Criticality:** low | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for dpia completion for high-risk processing aligned with APPI; PDPA Thailand.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APPI compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes privacy and sourcetypes dpia:register. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=privacy sourcetype="dpia:register" earliest=-730d risk_score>6
| stats latest(status) as st by processing_activity_id
| where st!="Completed"
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=privacy`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=dpia:register`.

## Visualization

Stacked bar (jurisdiction x action), Table (open reviews), Single value (exports without mechanism). Key fields: `processing_activity_id`, `st`.
