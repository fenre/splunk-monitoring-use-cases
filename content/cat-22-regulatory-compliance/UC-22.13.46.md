# 22.13.46 — Recovery Plan Exercise Attendance and Scenario Evidence

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We log who ran tabletop or functional recovery exercises and which BES systems were exercised, supporting recovery plan testing evidence.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes cip009_exercise, cip009_exercise`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype=cip009_exercise earliest=-400d
| eval ex_epoch=strptime(exercise_date,"%Y-%m-%d")
| eval annual_cutoff=relative_time(now(),"-1y@d")
| stats latest(exercise_type) as last_type latest(exercise_date) as last_date values(participant) as people by bes_system
| eval compliant=if(strptime(last_date,"%Y-%m-%d")>=annual_cutoff,1,0)
| where compliant=0
| table bes_system, last_date, last_type, people
```

## Implementation

(1) Push exercise completion events from GRC. (2) Align `bes_system` with CIP-002 list. (3) Alert before annual due. (4) Attach scenario documents out-of-band with links in events. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cip009_exercise`, `sourcetype=cip009_exercise``.
