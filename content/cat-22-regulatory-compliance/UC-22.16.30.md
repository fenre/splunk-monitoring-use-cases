## Description

## Value

## SPL

```spl
index=dns sourcetype=infoblox:dns view="OT" earliest=-7d
| eval uc_tag="22.16.30"
| lookup threat_intel_domains.csv query OUTPUT intel_severity
| where isnotnull(intel_severity)
| stats count by query, src_ip
```
