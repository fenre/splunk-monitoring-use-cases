# 22.33.8 — SAMA cybersecurity incident reporting timeline

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Aligns SAMA Cyber Security Framework supervisory expectations with measurable Splunk evidence for sama cybersecurity incident reporting timeline.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SAMA CSF compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes training and sourcetypes sama:awareness. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=training sourcetype="sama:awareness" earliest=-365d
| stats latest(completion_pct) as p by employee_class
| where p<95
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize bilingual field extractions in props.conf where required. (2) Map IP ranges and business units to national critical infrastructure and banking registers. (3) Route SAMA, QCB, NESA, and PDPL alerts to regional bilingual runbooks. (4) Weekly leadership digest with regulator-specific views. Indexes required: `index=training`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=sama:awareness`.
