# UC-22.11.85 · Formal Risk Assessment Evidence and Residual Risk Score Trend

## Description

Tracks annual enterprise risk assessment submissions including PCI-scoped risk items and residual scores so targeted risk analyses required by the standard are current and leadership-reviewed.

## Value

Tracks annual enterprise risk assessment submissions including PCI-scoped risk items and residual scores so targeted risk analyses required by the standard are current and leadership-reviewed.

## Implementation

(1) Align `review_date` to annual cycle. (2) Map `score` scale 1–25 consistently. (3) Escalate `past_due` to risk committee. (4) Export closed reviews for PCI policy pack.

Step 1 — Configure data collection

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=grc sourcetype="grc:risk_register" earliest=-730d
| where pci_relevant="true"
| eval overdue=if(now()>strptime(review_date,"%Y-%m-%d"),1,0)
| stats latest(residual_score) as score, max(overdue) as past_due by risk_id, title
| where past_due=1 OR score>15
| sort score
```
