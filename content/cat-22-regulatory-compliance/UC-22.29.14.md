# 22.29.14 — PIPL cybersecurity protection obligations monitoring

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for pipl cybersecurity protection obligations monitoring aligned with PDPA SG; PIPL; K-ISMS.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SG PDPA compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes o365 and sourcetypes ms:o365:management. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=o365 sourcetype="ms:o365:management" Workload=SharePoint earliest=-7d
| stats dc(SiteUrl) as sites by UserId, Operation
| where Operation="FileDownloaded" AND sites>40
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=o365`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ms:o365:management`.

## Visualization

Stacked bar (jurisdiction x action), Table (open reviews), Single value (exports without mechanism). Key fields: `Operation`, `UserId`, `sites`.
