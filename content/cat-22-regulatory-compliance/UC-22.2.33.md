# UC-22.2.33 · NIS2 Incident Reporting Timeline Compliance (24h Early Warning / 72h Notification)

## Description

National transpositions specify early warning and incident notification deadlines. This use case extends UC-22.2.1 by explicitly clocking awareness time, regulatory filing timestamps, and CSIRT handoff fields — separating internal detection latency from legal notification compliance.

## Value

National transpositions specify early warning and incident notification deadlines. This use case extends UC-22.2.1 by explicitly clocking awareness time, regulatory filing timestamps, and CSIRT handoff fields — separating internal detection latency from legal notification compliance.

## Implementation

(1) Train analysts to record structured timestamps in `status_description` or use custom fields via adaptive response; (2) map incident classes to which deadlines apply per national law; (3) alert legal at T-2h before each deadline; (4) retain immutable export of closed incidents; (5) integrate with UC-22.2.19 for cross-border cases.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
`notable` urgency IN ("high","critical") earliest=-14d
| eval awareness_time=strptime(status_description,"(?i)awareness[:\\s]+(%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S)")
| eval early_warning_sent=strptime(status_description,"(?i)early.warning[:\\s]+(%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S)")
| eval notification_72h=strptime(status_description,"(?i)72h.notification[:\\s]+(%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S)")
| eval hours_to_early=round((early_warning_sent-awareness_time)/3600,2)
| eval hours_to_72=round((notification_72h-awareness_time)/3600,2)
| where hours_to_early > 24 OR hours_to_72 > 72 OR isnull(early_warning_sent)
| table rule_name, awareness_time, early_warning_sent, notification_72h, hours_to_early, hours_to_72
```
