# 22.16.29 — First-seen OT patch-repository binary hashes

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "First-seen OT patch-repository binary hashes".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes os and sourcetypes stash:file_integrity. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=os sourcetype=stash:file_integrity path="*OT_Patch_Repo*" earliest=-90d
| eval uc_tag="22.16.29"
| stats earliest(_time) as first_seen by sha256, path
| eventstats earliest(first_seen) as g by sha256
| where first_seen=g AND first_seen > relative_time(now(),"-30d@d")
```
