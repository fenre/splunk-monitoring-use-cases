# UC-22.29.23 · Regulatory consultation trigger monitoring

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for regulatory consultation trigger monitoring aligned with APPI; PDPA Thailand.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APPI compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ticketing and sourcetypes snow:sc_req_item. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=ticketing`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=snow:sc_req_item`.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=ticketing sourcetype="snow:sc_req_item" cat_item="*DPIA*" earliest=-180d
| eval opened=strptime(opened_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval closed=strptime(closed_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval age=round((now()-opened)/86400,0)
| where isnull(closed) AND age>60
```
