# 22.22.2 — SCA Exemption Usage Tracking (RTS on SCA & CSC)

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Monitors PSD2 strong customer authentication telemetry against documented baselines to evidence RTS compliance and proportionate exemption usage.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes payments and sourcetypes psd2:sca, psd2:sca`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=payments sourcetype="psd2:sca" earliest=-24h
| eval sca_dim="exemption"
| eval challenge_flag=if(challenge="true" OR challenge="1",1,0)
| stats count as authn, sum(eval(outcome="success")) as ok, sum(challenge_flag) as ch, dc(psu_id) as psu by aspsp_id, sca_dim
| lookup psd2_sca_baseline.csv aspsp_id OUTPUT challenge_rate_baseline AS baseline
| eval challenge_rate=round(100*ch/authn,3)
| eval delta=round(challenge_rate-baseline,3)
| eval uc_id="22.22.2"
| where authn>100 AND (delta>2 OR delta<-2)
| table uc_id, aspsp_id, sca_dim, authn, challenge_rate, baseline, delta
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Authentication.Authentication by Authentication.action, Authentication.user, Authentication.src | sort - count
```
