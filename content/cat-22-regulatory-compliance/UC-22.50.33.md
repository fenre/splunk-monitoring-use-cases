# 22.50.33 — COSO Principle 11 — Technology general controls: IT change-management and configuration-drift monitoring

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors change management and configuration drift for COSO Principle 11 (general controls over technology). Separates change ticket approval tracking from configuration drift detection, fixing the math that previously confounded the two event types.

## Value

COSO Principle 11 requires selection and development of general controls over technology. Continuous change-management and configuration-drift monitoring provides auditable evidence that IT general controls are effective.

## SPL

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype IN (change_tickets, config_drift) earliest=-30d
| eval is_change=if(sourcetype="change_tickets", 1, 0)
| eval is_drift=if(sourcetype="config_drift", 1, 0)
| eval approved_change=if(is_change=1 AND approval_status="approved", 1, 0)
| eval unauthorized_change=if(is_change=1 AND approval_status!="approved", 1, 0)
| stats sum(is_change) AS total_changes sum(approved_change) AS approved sum(unauthorized_change) AS unauthorized sum(is_drift) AS drift_events dc(host) AS affected_hosts BY business_unit
| eval approval_rate=if(total_changes>0, round(100*approved/total_changes, 1), 100)
| eval risk_level=case(unauthorized>0 OR drift_events>5, "high", drift_events>0, "medium", true(), "low")
| where risk_level!="low"
| table business_unit, total_changes, approved, unauthorized, approval_rate, drift_events, affected_hosts, risk_level
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest change-ticket and config-drift events with change_ticket_ref, approval_status, change_type; (2) correlate drift with change tickets; (3) schedule daily; (4) escalate unauthorised changes. Indexes required: `index=change_mgmt`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=change_tickets`, `sourcetype=config_drift`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `affected_assets`, `approval_pct`, `approved`, `approved_changes`, `change_category`, `emergency`.

## Compliance

- **COSO** 2013 ICFR: Principle11 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Principle11.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
