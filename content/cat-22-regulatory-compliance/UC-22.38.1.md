# 22.38.1 — Cross-border personal-data flow anomaly — egress to unsanctioned jurisdictions

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** verified

## Description

Joins CIM Network_Traffic egress telemetry with DLP PII classification and a sanctioned-jurisdictions lookup. Any transfer of PII-classified traffic to a non-sanctioned destination is a direct Art.44 finding.

## Value

Operationalises a control that is otherwise verifiable only via quarterly desk review of the transfer register.

## SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic where All_Traffic.tag=egress earliest=-1h by All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.bytes_out
| `get_geolocation(dest)`
| lookup sanctioned_transfer_jurisdictions.csv country as dest_country OUTPUT scc_active adequacy_decision
| where isnull(scc_active) AND isnull(adequacy_decision)
| join src [search index=dlp tag=pii | stats values(classification) as pii_class by src]
| table _time src dest dest_country pii_class bytes_out
```

## Implementation

(1) Ensure DLP emits CIM-compatible PII classification; (2) maintain sanctioned_transfer_jurisdictions.csv driven by the Art.45 adequacy list and the SCC register; (3) schedule every 15 min; (4) escalate via SOAR to DPO. Indexes required: `index=dlp`.

## Visualization

World-map of egress flows (green=sanctioned, red=finding), table of findings, single-value 'transfers in sanctioned geographies'. Key fields: `bytes_out`, `dest`, `dest_country`, `pii_class`, `src`, `src]`.

## Compliance

- **GDPR** 2016/679: Art.44 (full) — Art.44 prohibits transfers to third countries absent Art.45/46/49 safeguards; the UC detects egress to jurisdictions not on the adequacy list or SCC register.
- **GDPR** 2016/679: Art.46 (partial) — Art.46 Standard Contractual Clauses — the UC evidences that transfers only occur to destinations registered as SCC-covered.
- **UK GDPR** post-Brexit: Art.44 (partial) — Derived from GDPR@2016/679 Art.44 (parent assurance='full'). UK GDPR preserves parent clause numbering; inherited assurance degraded one step.
- **UK GDPR** post-Brexit: Art.46 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for Art.46.

## Known False Positives

Corporate VPN endpoints that present a destination IP in a non-sanctioned country can mis-geolocate; add vpn_exits.csv to suppress.
