# 22.30.21 — Policy framework compliance monitoring

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports APRA CPS 234 supervisory examination readiness for policy framework compliance monitoring.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APRA CPS 234 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes audit and sourcetypes cps234:policy_ack. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=audit sourcetype="cps234:policy_ack" earliest=-365d
| stats latest(ack) as a by policy_id, board_member
| where a!="true"
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t latest(All_Changes.status) as agg_value from datamodel=Change.All_Changes by All_Changes.action, All_Changes.object_category, All_Changes.user | sort - agg_value
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_material_services.csv` for important business services and regulator tags. (2) Send weekly digests to CRO and technology risk committees. (3) Validate DB Connect concurrency for audit peak windows. (4) Archive results to a restricted summary index for retention governance. Indexes required: `index=audit`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cps234:policy_ack`.
