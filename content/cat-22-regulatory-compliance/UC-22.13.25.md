# 22.13.25 — Visitor Badge Without Escort Within Policy Window

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We find visitor badge reads that lack a proximate escort badge swipe, supporting visitor control procedures.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes physical and sourcetypes pacs:access, pacs:access`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=physical sourcetype=pacs:access badge_type="visitor" earliest=-7d
| where isnull(escort_badge_id) OR escort_present="false"
| lookup cip_psp_register reader_id OUTPUT psp_name
| stats count by visitor_id, reader_id, psp_name
| where count>=1
| table visitor_id, reader_id, psp_name, count
```
