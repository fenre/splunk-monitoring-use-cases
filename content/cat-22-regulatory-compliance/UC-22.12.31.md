# UC-22.12.31 · Audit finding remediation milestone and due-date risk

## Description

Supports SOX IT general and application controls evidence for "Audit finding remediation milestone and due-date risk" by correlating security, change, ERP, and ITSM telemetry with control ownership records, strengthening SOX remediation attestation.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SOX ITGC compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsm and sourcetypes snow:problem. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=itsm`.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype=snow:problem short_description="*audit finding*" earliest=-730d
| eval uc_tag="22.12.31"
| eval due=strptime(due_date,"%Y-%m-%d")
| where due < relative_time(now(),"+30d@d") AND state!="Closed"
| table number, short_description, due_date, assignment_group
```
