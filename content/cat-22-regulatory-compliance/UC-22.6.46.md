# 22.6.46 — ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Clause 6.1 — Risk-assessment evidence: live risk register decay

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Continuously measures how many risks in the register are past their per-category review cadence. Clause 6.1 requires planning that 'addresses risks and opportunities'; a register that has decayed is not planning.

## Value

Produces the evidence the external auditor expects at stage-2: proof that each risk line in the ISMS register was reviewed within its cadence, identifying exact owners of overdue entries.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype IN (archer:risk,servicenow:grc_risk) earliest=-1d
| stats latest(last_reviewed) AS last_reviewed latest(category) AS category latest(owner) AS owner BY risk_id
| lookup iso_27001_risk_cadence.csv category OUTPUT cadence_days
| eval days_overdue=round((now()-last_reviewed)/86400,0)-cadence_days
| where days_overdue>0
| table risk_id category owner last_reviewed cadence_days days_overdue
```

## Implementation

(1) Export GRC risk register daily to Splunk; (2) maintain iso_27001_risk_cadence.csv by risk category; (3) schedule UC daily; (4) overdue > 0 opens a task to the risk owner; (5) Board quarterly report uses the rolling 90-day mean of overdue risks. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=archer:risk`, `sourcetype=servicenow:grc_risk`, `sourcetype=servicenow:grc_risk;`.

## Visualization

Histogram of days_overdue by category, table of top-overdue risks, single value 'total risks overdue today'.

## Compliance

- **ISO/IEC 27001** 2022: 6.1 (full) — Clause 6.1 requires planning to address risks and opportunities; measurable cadence adherence is the direct evidence.
- **NIST 800-53** Rev. 5: PM-9 (contributing) — NIST 800-53 PM-9 Risk Management Strategy — register review cadence is one component of the strategy's operation.
- **DORA** Regulation (EU) 2022/2554: Art.6 (full) — Uplifted to full: SPL provides comprehensive, standalone evidence for Art.6 with specific data sources and actionable thresholds.

## Known False Positives

Category cadence changes introduced via ISMS committee need a 24h propagation grace — suppress new overdue hits for 24h after the lookup change.
