# 22.34.11 — Independent assessment finding remediation tracking

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** expert | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Produces SWIFT Customer Security Programme evidence for independent assessment finding remediation tracking ahead of independent assessment.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SWIFT CSP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes audit and sourcetypes swift:independent_assessment. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=audit sourcetype="swift:independent_assessment" earliest=-730d
| stats latest(severity) as sev, latest(remediation_status) as rs by finding_id
| where rs!="Closed" AND match(sev,"High|Critical")
| table finding_id, sev, rs
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Authentication.Authentication by Authentication.action, Authentication.user, Authentication.src | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Segment SWIFT CSP evidence into dedicated indexes with strict role-based access. (2) Join operators to HR screening and vendor risk lookups refreshed weekly. (3) Validate MFA and PAM sourcetypes are CIM-mapped for Authentication models. (4) Retain evidence for at least 13 months aligned to attestation cycles. Indexes required: `index=audit`.

## Visualization

## Known False Positives
