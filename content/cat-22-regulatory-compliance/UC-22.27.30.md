# 22.27.30 — Malware protection and update compliance for CE scope

**Criticality:** medium | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports UK Cyber Essentials supervisory expectations by correlating technical telemetry with governance records for malware protection and update compliance for ce scope.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for Cyber Essentials compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes crowdstrike, defender and sourcetypes DefenderATP:Alert, crowdstrike:hosts. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=defender sourcetype="DefenderATP:Alert" OR index=crowdstrike sourcetype="crowdstrike:hosts" earliest=-7d
| stats latest(last_seen) as ls, latest(rtr_state) as rtr by hostname
| eval stale=if((now()-ls)>604800,1,0)
| lookup cyberessentials_endpoints.csv hostname OUTPUT in_scope
| where in_scope="true" AND (stale=1 OR rtr!="enabled")
| table hostname, ls, rtr, stale
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Authentication.Authentication by Authentication.dest | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `uk_oes_inventory.csv` with OES identifiers, essential services, and CAF mappings. (2) Tag correlation searches with `nis_relevant` or `fca_relevant` for workflow routing. (3) Integrate IR ticketing milestones for competent authority and FCA clocks. (4) Monthly review with compliance, technology risk, and resilience forums. Indexes required: `index=crowdstrike`, `index=defender`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=DefenderATP:Alert`, `sourcetype=crowdstrike:hosts`.
