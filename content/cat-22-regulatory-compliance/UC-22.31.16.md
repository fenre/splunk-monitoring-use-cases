# 22.31.16 — Incident notification workflow within regulatory expectations

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Evidences APRA CPS 234 obligations for incident notification workflow within regulatory expectations using endpoint, identity, and cloud telemetry.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APRA CPS 234 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes cps234:incident_register. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype="cps234:incident_register" earliest=-30d
| eval elapsed=(now()-strptime(detected_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S"))/3600
| where isnull(apra_notification_at) AND elapsed>60
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Endpoint.Processes by Processes.process_name, Processes.user, Processes.dest | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest CrowdStrike policy posture via TA 5082 and map hosts to NDB and NZISM tiers. (2) Tune alerts with ACSD Essential Eight and APRA-aligned severities. (3) Coordinate privacy and security ownership for dual-reporting workflows. (4) Monthly export for privacy officer and prudential working groups. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=cps234:incident_register`.
