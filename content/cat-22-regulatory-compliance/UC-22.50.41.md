# 22.50.41 — UN R155 7.2.2.5 — Cyber attack monitoring: VSOC detection and response evidence

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors VSOC detection and incident response rates for vehicle cyber attacks, providing evidence of UN R155 7.2.2.5 capabilities for monitoring, detecting, and responding to cyber threats.

## Value

UN R155 requires OEMs to monitor, detect, and respond to cyber attacks across their vehicle fleet. Continuous VSOC monitoring evidence demonstrates operational security capabilities.

## SPL

```spl
(index=vsoc sourcetype IN (vehicle_ids, vehicle_security_event)) OR (index=incident_response sourcetype=vsoc_ir_tickets) earliest=-30d
| eval detected=if(sourcetype IN ("vehicle_ids", "vehicle_security_event") AND isnotnull(threat_category), 1, 0)
| eval responded=if(sourcetype="vsoc_ir_tickets" AND status IN ("acknowledged", "investigating", "resolved"), 1, 0)
| stats sum(detected) AS detections sum(responded) AS responses dc(vehicle_model) AS models_monitored dc(threat_category) AS threat_categories BY attack_vector
| eval response_rate=if(detections>0, round(100*responses/detections, 1), 0)
| where response_rate<95 OR detections=0
| table attack_vector, detections, responses, response_rate, models_monitored, threat_categories
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest VSOC detection feeds and vehicle IDS alerts; (2) correlate with incident response tickets; (3) schedule daily; (4) escalate low response rates. Indexes required: `index=incident_response`, `index=vsoc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=vehicle_ids`, `sourcetype=vehicle_security_event`, `sourcetype=vsoc_ir_tickets`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `attack_vector`, `detected`, `detections`, `models_monitored`, `responded`, `response_rate`.

## Compliance

- **UN R155** 2021: 7.2.2.5 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for 7.2.2.5.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
