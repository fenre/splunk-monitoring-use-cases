# 22.29.13 — Reasonable security measures per Singapore PDPA

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for reasonable security measures per singapore pdpa aligned with PDPA SG; PIPL; K-ISMS.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SG PDPA compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes os and sourcetypes WinEventLog:Security. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=os sourcetype="WinEventLog:Security" EventCode=4663 earliest=-24h
| stats count by Object_Name, user
| lookup pdpa_sg_technical_measure.csv Object_Name OUTPUT requires_encryption
| where requires_encryption="true" AND count>50
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=os`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=WinEventLog:Security`.
