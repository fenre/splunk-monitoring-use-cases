# 22.29.22 — DPO activity reporting metrics

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for dpo activity reporting metrics aligned with APPI; PDPA Thailand.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for APPI compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes privacy and sourcetypes dpo:activity. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=privacy sourcetype="dpo:activity" earliest=-90d
| stats count by dpo_id, activity_type
| where activity_type="RegulatorConsultation" AND count<1
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=privacy`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=dpo:activity`.
