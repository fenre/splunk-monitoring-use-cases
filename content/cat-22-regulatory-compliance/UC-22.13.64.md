# 22.13.64 — Vendor Risk Assessment Due Dates for Critical Suppliers

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

We track vendor security risk assessments for suppliers touching BES Cyber Systems and alert before assessments expire.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NERC CIP compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes vendor:risk, vendor:risk`. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype=vendor:risk critical_vendor=1 earliest=-730d
| eval due=strptime(assessment_due,"%Y-%m-%d")
| eval days_left=round((due-now())/86400,0)
| where days_left < 30 OR isnull(assessment_completed)
| table vendor_id, vendor_name, assessment_due, days_left, owner
| sort days_left
```

## Implementation

(1) Sync vendor register from TPRM tool. (2) Alert at ninety, thirty, and seven days. (3) Block new access for expired high-risk vendors per policy. (4) Export for supply chain committee. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=vendor:risk`, `sourcetype=vendor:risk``.
