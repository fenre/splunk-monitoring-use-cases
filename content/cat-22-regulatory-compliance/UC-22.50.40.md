# 22.50.40 — UN R155 7.2.2.2 — Vehicle cybersecurity risk assessment and mitigation monitoring

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors TARA (Threat Analysis and Risk Assessment) results and mitigation status across vehicle platforms per UN R155 7.2.2.2 requirements for cybersecurity risk management.

## Value

UN R155 requires OEMs to perform risk assessments for vehicle cybersecurity threats. Continuous tracking of TARA results and mitigation status provides auditable evidence of the CSMS risk management process.

## SPL

```spl
index=vsoc sourcetype IN (tara_assessment, vehicle_vuln_tracker) earliest=-90d
| eval risk_mitigated=if(mitigation_status IN ("mitigated", "accepted", "transferred"), 1, 0)
| eval risk_level=case(cvss_score>=9.0, "critical", cvss_score>=7.0, "high", cvss_score>=4.0, "medium", true(), "low")
| stats count AS total_risks sum(risk_mitigated) AS mitigated dc(vehicle_platform) AS platforms_assessed dc(ecu_component) AS components_assessed latest(assessment_date) AS last_tara BY vehicle_platform, risk_level
| eval unmitigated=total_risks - mitigated
| eval mitigation_pct=round(100*mitigated/total_risks, 1)
| where unmitigated>0
| table vehicle_platform, risk_level, total_risks, mitigated, unmitigated, mitigation_pct, components_assessed, last_tara
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest TARA results and vulnerability tracker data from VSOC; (2) normalise cvss_score, mitigation_status, vehicle_platform, ecu_component; (3) schedule monthly; (4) escalate unmitigated critical risks. Indexes required: `index=vsoc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=tara_assessment`, `sourcetype=vehicle_vuln_tracker`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `components_assessed`, `last_tara`, `mitigated`, `mitigation_pct`, `platforms_assessed`, `risk_level`.

## Compliance

- **UN R155** 2021: 7.2.2.2 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for 7.2.2.2.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
