# 22.20.16 — CMMC assessment readiness — artifact 1

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Demonstrates contractor cybersecurity hygiene for "CMMC assessment readiness — artifact 1" using CUI-scoped telemetry mapped to CMMC 2.0 Assessment expectations.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for CMMC compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes governance and sourcetypes cmmc:self_assess. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=governance sourcetype=cmmc:self_assess earliest=-180d
| eval uc_tag="22.20.16"
| stats latest(score) as score by practice_id
| where score < 0.85
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Endpoint.Processes by Processes.process_name, Processes.user, Processes.dest | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=governance`.

## Visualization

Stacked bar, Table, Time chart, Single value. Key fields: `practice_id`, `score`, `uc_tag`.
