# UC-22.5.15 · MiFID II Conflicts of Interest — Personal Account Dealing Near Client Block Trades

## Description

Conflicts policies require surveillance of staff dealing. Correlating employee PA trades with adjacent client block orders in the same instruments surfaces potential front-running or misuse of confidential information.

## Value

Conflicts policies require surveillance of staff dealing. Correlating employee PA trades with adjacent client block orders in the same instruments surfaces potential front-running or misuse of confidential information.

## Implementation

(1) Tune the 900-second proximity window per liquidity and venue; (2) same-side matching surfaces potential front-running—adjust to opposite-side if your policy targets different abuse patterns; (3) enforce strict RBAC on `staff_id`; (4) escalate hits to compliance investigations with underlying fills retained under legal hold outside Splunk; (5) document methodology in the conflicts register.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=surveillance sourcetype="pad:trade" earliest=-90d
| rename side as pad_side
| eval t=strptime(trade_ts,"%Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%SZ")
| join type=left symbol [
    search index=trading sourcetype="oms:block" earliest=-90d
    | rename side as block_side
    | eval bt=strptime(block_ts,"%Y-%m-%dT%H:%M:%SZ")
    | fields symbol, bt, block_side, qty
  ]
| eval delta=abs(t-bt)
| where delta<900 AND pad_side=block_side
| table staff_id, symbol, pad_side, t, bt, delta, qty
```
