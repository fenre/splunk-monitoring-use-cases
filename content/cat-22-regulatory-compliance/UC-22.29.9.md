# 22.29.9 — Affected individual notification evidence

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Provides APAC privacy supervisory evidence for affected individual notification evidence aligned with APAC breach laws.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for SG PDPA, AU Privacy Act, PIPL compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes privacy and sourcetypes breach:event. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=privacy sourcetype="breach:event" jurisdiction IN ("SG","TH","JP","KR","CN") earliest=-180d
| eval detected=strptime(detected_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval authority=strptime(notified_authority_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval individuals=strptime(notified_individuals_at,"%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S")
| eval sla_h=case(jurisdiction="SG",72,jurisdiction="TH",72,jurisdiction="JP",120,1=1,24+9)
| eval breach=if(isnull(authority) AND (now()-detected)>sla_h*3600,1,0)
| eval register_line="9"
| table breach_id, jurisdiction, breach, register_line
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `apac_transfer_register.csv` and consent or DPIA lookups referenced in runbooks. (2) Map technical identifiers to records of processing activities with legal review. (3) Restrict indexes to regional privacy roles with least privilege. (4) Quarterly DPIA and consent linkage review with local counsel. Indexes required: `index=privacy`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=breach:event`.
