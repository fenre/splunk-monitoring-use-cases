# UC-22.11.103 · PCI-DSS 11.3 — Vulnerability programme: overdue scan cadence and unremediated high-severity

## Description

Produces, per scan scope, the evidence required by PCI-DSS 11.3 — scans are on cadence and high/critical findings are remediated within SLA.

## Value

Closes the #1 open-finding reason at QSAs — 'the scan history was incomplete'. Continuous measurement is the intended replacement.

## Implementation

(1) Normalise scanner feeds; (2) maintain pci_scan_scope.csv (quarterly external, per-change internal); (3) schedule UC daily; (4) sla_met=0 opens a P2; (5) quarterly QSA report.

## Detailed Implementation

Step 1 — Configure data collection
(1) Normalise scanner feeds; (2) maintain pci_scan_scope.csv (quarterly external, per-change internal); (3) schedule UC daily; (4) sla_met=0 opens a P2; (5) quarterly QSA report.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=vm sourcetype IN (tenable:sc:vuln,qualys:host) earliest=-120d
| stats latest(_time) AS last_scan_at count(eval(severity IN ("high","critical") AND state="open")) AS open_high_findings avg(eval(if(state="open" AND severity IN ("high","critical"),now()-first_seen,null))) AS mean_age_s BY scan_scope
| eval mean_age_days=round(mean_age_s/86400,0)
| lookup pci_scan_scope.csv scan_scope OUTPUT cadence_days sla_days
| eval sla_met=if(last_scan_at>=now()-cadence_days*86400 AND mean_age_days<=sla_days,1,0)
| where sla_met=0
| table scan_scope last_scan_at sla_met open_high_findings mean_age_days
```
