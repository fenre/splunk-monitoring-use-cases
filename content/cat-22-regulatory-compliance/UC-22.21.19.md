# 22.21.19 — CE Marking Evidence — Test Report Coverage (Art. 48)

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Joins product_sku to test_report_id coverage from test lab feeds for CE marking evidentiary chain.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes plm, product, testlab and sourcetypes lab:report, lab:report`, plm:sku. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=plm sourcetype="plm:sku" earliest=-1d
| fields product_sku, model_family, hw_rev
| join type=left product_sku [
    search index=testlab sourcetype="lab:report" earliest=-400d
    | stats latest(report_id) as test_report_id by product_sku
  ]
| eval covered=if(isnotnull(test_report_id),1,0)
| stats sum(covered) as ok, count as total
| eval coverage_pct=round(100*ok/total,2)
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize SKU keys; (2) nightly DB Connect sync; (3) alert coverage_pct < 100 for EU SKUs; (4) map to notified body references outside Splunk. Indexes required: `index=plm`, `index=product`, `index=testlab`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=lab:report`, `sourcetype=lab:report``, `sourcetype=plm:sku`, `sourcetype=plm:sku``.

## Visualization
