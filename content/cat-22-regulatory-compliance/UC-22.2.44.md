# UC-22.2.44 · NIS2 Cooperation Group and Sector Information Sharing Participation

## Description

Cooperation mechanisms and information sharing strengthen collective resilience. This use case logs participation in sector ISACs, national cooperation groups, and Splunk-fed threat intel exchanges — tracking attendance, indicator contributions, and action items.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for NIS2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsm, threat_intel. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Register each cooperation forum with expected meeting cadence; (2) track attendance via ServiceNow tasks; (3) measure threat feed health from Splunk intel indexes; (4) alert CISO when participation drops; (5) document confidentiality constraints on shared data. Indexes required: `index=itsm`, `index=threat_intel`; Lookups: `nis2_cooperation_group_register`, `nis2_cooperation_group_register.csv`.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
| inputlookup nis2_cooperation_group_register.csv
| join group_id [
    search index=itsm short_description="*ISAC*" OR short_description="*cooperation group*" earliest=-180d
    | stats latest(state) as last_meeting_state latest(closed_at) as last_meeting_date by assignment_group
    | rename assignment_group as group_id
]
| eval participation_gap=if(last_meeting_state!="Closed" OR isnull(last_meeting_date),1,0)
| join group_id [
    search index=threat_intel earliest=-30d
    | stats dc(indicator_id) as indicators_consumed by feed_name
    | rename feed_name as group_id
]
| where participation_gap=1 OR indicators_consumed < 1000
| table group_id, last_meeting_state, last_meeting_date, indicators_consumed, participation_gap
```
