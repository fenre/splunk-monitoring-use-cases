## Value

## SPL

```spl
index=gxp sourcetype=eln:signature earliest=-30d
| eval uc_tag="22.17.10"
| transaction signer_id maxspan=120s
| eval steps=mvcount(signature_step)
| where steps < 3 AND match(document_type,"(?i)release")
```
