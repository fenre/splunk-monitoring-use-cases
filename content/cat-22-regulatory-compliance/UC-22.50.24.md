# 22.50.24 — Basel III OPR25.1 — Operational risk event capture and loss-data aggregation

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Aggregates operational risk events, losses, and near-misses across business lines per OPR25.1 requirements for operational risk identification, assessment, monitoring, and reporting.

## Value

OPR25.1 mandates banks to identify, assess, monitor, and control/mitigate operational risk. Continuous loss-data aggregation provides the evidence base for the operational risk management framework.

## SPL

```spl
index=risk_events sourcetype IN (oprisk_events, incident_mgmt) earliest=-30d
| eval loss_amount=coalesce(loss_amount, 0)
| eval risk_category=coalesce(risk_category, "unclassified")
| stats count AS event_count sum(loss_amount) AS total_loss dc(business_unit) AS affected_units values(risk_category) AS categories BY business_line, event_type
| eval severity=case(total_loss>=1000000, "critical", total_loss>=100000, "high", total_loss>=10000, "medium", true(), "low")
| where event_count>0
| sort -total_loss
| table business_line, event_type, event_count, total_loss, severity, affected_units, categories
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest operational risk events from GRC/incident management platforms; (2) normalise loss_amount and risk_category fields; (3) schedule daily; (4) route critical-severity events to risk committee dashboard. Indexes required: `index=risk_events`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=incident_mgmt`, `sourcetype=oprisk_events`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `affected_units`, `business_line`, `categories`, `event_count`, `event_type`, `loss_amount`.

## Compliance

- **Basel III** BCBS 2021: OPR25.1 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for OPR25.1.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
