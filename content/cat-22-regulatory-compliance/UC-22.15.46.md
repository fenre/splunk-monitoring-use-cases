# 22.15.46 — Zone Boundary Traffic Monitoring — Anomalous Inter-Zone Volume

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Baselines permitted bytes and sessions between OT zones and flags spikes that may indicate policy drift, covert channels, or misrouted traffic at conduits.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for IEC 62443 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes fw and sourcetypes pan:traffic. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=fw sourcetype="pan:traffic" earliest=-14d
| eval zone_pair=concat(src_zone,"->",dest_zone)
| bin _time span=1h
| stats sum(bytes) as b, dc(dest_port) as ports by _time, zone_pair, app
| eventstats median(b) as med by zone_pair, app
| eval ratio=if(med>0, round(b/med,2), null())
| where ratio>8 AND b>10485760
| sort - b
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t dc(All_Traffic.dest_port) as agg_value from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.app span=1h | sort - agg_value
```

## Implementation

(1) Build `ot_zone_pairs_expected.csv` for pairs that should exist; (2) exclude known maintenance windows; (3) alert on new `app` on sensitive pairs; (4) correlate with change records. Indexes required: `index=fw`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=pan:traffic`.

## Visualization

## Known False Positives
