# UC-22.25.17 ┬и Periodic KYC Review Compliance and Overdue Reviews

## Description

Strengthens anti-money laundering and counter-terrorist financing monitoring, investigations, and supervisory examination readiness across transactions, customers, and list screening.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AML compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes aml and sourcetypes kyc:review:schedule. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=aml`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=kyc:review:schedule`.

Step 2 Рђћ Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=aml sourcetype="kyc:review:schedule" earliest=-7d
| eval aml_tag="pkr"
| eval uc_id="22.25.17"
| bin _time span=1h
| stats count as events, sum(eval(screening_result="hit")) as hits, median(screening_latency_ms) as lat by customer_id, aml_tag, _time
| eventstats perc95(amount_eur) as p95_amt by aml_tag
| where (aml_tag="struct" AND amount_eur>9000 AND amount_eur<10000) OR hits>0 OR lat>800
| sort - events
| head 300
```
