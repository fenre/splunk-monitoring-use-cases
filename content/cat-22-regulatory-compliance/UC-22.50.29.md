# 22.50.29 — COPPA §312.3 — Verifiable parental consent: consent-record completeness and audit trail

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** verified

## Description

Monitors child-account registrations and verifies that verifiable parental consent records exist per §312.3 requirements, flagging accounts lacking valid consent documentation.

## Value

§312.3 requires operators to obtain verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information from children under 13. Continuous monitoring ensures no child account operates without documented consent.

## SPL

```spl
index=user_mgmt sourcetype IN (registration, consent_records) earliest=-30d
| search user_age_bracket="under_13" OR account_type="child"
| eval has_parental_consent=if(isnotnull(parental_consent_date) AND parental_consent_method IN ("signed_form", "credit_card_verification", "government_id", "video_call", "knowledge_based"), 1, 0)
| eval consent_valid=if(has_parental_consent=1 AND parental_consent_date<=_time, 1, 0)
| stats count AS child_accounts sum(consent_valid) AS with_valid_consent dc(eval(if(consent_valid=0, user_id, null()))) AS missing_consent_users BY service, registration_source
| eval consent_coverage_pct=round(100*with_valid_consent/child_accounts, 1)
| where consent_coverage_pct<100
| table service, registration_source, child_accounts, with_valid_consent, missing_consent_users, consent_coverage_pct
```

## Implementation

(1) Ingest registration and consent-management logs with user_age_bracket, parental_consent_date, parental_consent_method; (2) schedule daily; (3) immediately flag accounts without consent for remediation. Indexes required: `index=user_mgmt`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=consent_records`, `sourcetype=registration`.

## Visualization

Table of flagged records for investigation; single-value KPI of coverage / compliance percentage; time chart of trend over the last 90 days; drill-down per responsible owner. Key fields: `child_accounts`, `consent_coverage_pct`, `consent_valid`, `has_parental_consent`, `missing_consent_users`, `registration_source`.

## Compliance

- **COPPA** 16 CFR 312: §312.3 (partial) — Uplifted from contributing to partial: UC provides meaningful monitoring evidence for §312.3.

## Known False Positives

Tuning prerequisites: source field mappings must be in place before the UC will produce reliable metrics; early runs will commonly flag unmapped systems until onboarding is completed.
