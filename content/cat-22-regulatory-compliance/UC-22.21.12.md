# 22.21.12 — Escalation to Human Review Queue Latency (Art. 14)

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Tracks SLA from model_low_confidence event to human_assigned for timely human review per oversight procedures.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for EU AI Act compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes ai_hil and sourcetypes ai:human:queue. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=ai_hil sourcetype="ai:human:queue" earliest=-7d (event="low_confidence" OR event="human_assigned")
| eval t=if(event="low_confidence",_time,null())
| eval t2=if(event="human_assigned",_time,null())
| stats min(t) as t_lc, min(t2) as t_ha by decision_id
| eval sla_sec=t_ha-t_lc
| where isnotnull(t_lc) AND (isnull(t_ha) OR sla_sec>900)
| eval breach=if(isnull(t_ha),"unassigned",if(sla_sec>900,"late","ok"))
| table decision_id, t_lc, t_ha, sla_sec, breach
```

## Implementation

(1) Unify timestamps to UTC; (2) set SLA based on risk class; (3) ITSI threshold on median sla_sec; (4) playbook for reassignment. Indexes required: `index=ai_hil`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=ai:human:queue`.
