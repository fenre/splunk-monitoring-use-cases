# 22.22.28 — Payment Service Availability SLO Breach Tracking

**Criticality:** high | **Difficulty:** intermediate | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports incident governance, customer communications evidence, and supervisory reporting workflows associated with payment service continuity and fraud statistics.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for PSD2 compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes payments and sourcetypes pay:health:probe. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=payments sourcetype="pay:health:probe" earliest=-7d
| eval inc_tag="slo"
| bin _time span=5m
| stats avg(availability_pct) as avgp, min(availability_pct) as min_av by aspsp_id, payment_scheme, _time
| eval uc_id="22.22.28"
| where avgp < 99.5 OR min_av < 99.0
| sort _time
| table uc_id, _time, aspsp_id, payment_scheme, avgp, min_av
```

## Implementation

(1) Normalize field extractions and CIM tags; (2) schedule hourly or near-real-time; (3) route alerts to SOC/compliance queues; (4) export evidence packs for supervisory review. Indexes required: `index=payments`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=pay:health:probe`.

## Visualization
