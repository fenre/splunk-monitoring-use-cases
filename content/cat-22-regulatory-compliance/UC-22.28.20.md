# 22.28.20 — Critical infrastructure reporting for insurance sector

**Criticality:** critical | **Difficulty:** beginner | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Demonstrates alignment with BAIT/KAIT expectations for critical infrastructure reporting for insurance sector using auditable Splunk evidence.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for BAIT/KAIT compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes grc and sourcetypes kritis:finance_report. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=grc sourcetype="kritis:finance_report" earliest=-365d
| stats latest(submitted) as sub by institution_id, period
| where sub!="true"
| table institution_id, period, sub
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t latest(All_Changes.status) as agg_value from datamodel=Change.All_Changes by All_Changes.action, All_Changes.object_category, All_Changes.user | sort - agg_value
```

## Implementation

(1) Maintain `de_kritis_assets.csv` with sector classification and KRITIS identifiers. (2) Link ITSI services to KRITIS objects for business impact overlays. (3) Export monthly control posture snapshots for BSI engagement. (4) Tune thresholds with enterprise risk management and OT security owners. Indexes required: `index=grc`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=kritis:finance_report`.
