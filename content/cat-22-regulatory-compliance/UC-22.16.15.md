# 22.16.15 — Composite severity from OT alerts and SCADA health

**Criticality:** low | **Difficulty:** advanced | **Status:** N/A

## Description

Supports pipeline cybersecurity program oversight under TSA Pipeline Security Directive by correlating operational telemetry with governance records for "Composite severity from OT alerts and SCADA health".

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for TSA SD compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsi_summary, ot and sourcetypes nozomi:alert. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## SPL

```spl
index=itsi_summary earliest=-7d service_title="*Pipeline SCADA*"
| eval uc_tag="22.16.15"
| append [ search index=ot sourcetype=nozomi:alert earliest=-7d | eval w=if(severity="critical",4,2) | stats sum(w) as alert_w by site_name ]
| stats max(health_score) as hs, max(alert_w) as aw by site_name
```

## CIM SPL

```spl
| tstats summariesonly=t count from datamodel=Network_Traffic.All_Traffic by All_Traffic.action, All_Traffic.src, All_Traffic.dest, All_Traffic.dest_port | sort - count
```

## Implementation

(1) Confirm field extractions and CIM tags for the sourcetypes in scope; (2) Load or maintain the referenced lookups/KVStore collections with owner attestation dates; (3) Schedule the search at an interval aligned to the control’s materiality; (4) Route positive findings to the compliance ticketing queue with required evidence fields; (5) Retain scheduled search exports per records management and legal hold procedures. Indexes required: `index=itsi_summary`, `index=ot`.

## Visualization

Time chart, Choropleth or site bar, Table, Single value. Key fields: `]`, `alert_w`, `aw`, `hs`, `site_name`, `uc_tag`.
