# UC-22.5.5 · MiFID II Product Governance and Target Market Appropriateness Evidence

## Description

Manufacturers and distributors must maintain product-approval processes and identify target markets. Tracking workflow completion for new product launches evidences governance discipline and helps detect rushed approvals or missing distributor notifications.

## Value

Gives the responsible team auditor-ready evidence for MiFID II compliance by automating regulatory compliance evidence using indexes itsm and sourcetypes snow:sc_req_item. Replaces manual evidence gathering with a continuous Splunk search that produces structured, time-stamped artefacts suitable for regulatory examination and internal audit review.

## Implementation

(1) Model ServiceNow catalog items for product approval with mandatory `u_target_market_signed_off`; (2) require `u_product_isin` or internal SKU; (3) alert on `closed_without_signoff>0`; (4) join marketing distribution lists optional via lookup; (5) archive closed items quarterly for NCAs. Indexes required: `index=itsm`; Sourcetypes: `sourcetype=snow:sc_req_item`.

## Detailed Implementation

Prerequisites

• Ensure the following data sources are available: `index=itsm` `sourcetype="snow:sc_req_item"` (number, cat_item, state, opened_at, closed_at, u_product_isin, u_target_market_signed_off).
• For app installation, inputs.conf, and Splunk directory layout, see the Implementation guide: docs/implementation-guide.md

Step 1 — Configure data collection
(1) Model ServiceNow catalog items for product approval with mandatory `u_target_market_signed_off`; (2) require `u_product_isin` or internal SKU; (3) alert on `closed_without_signoff>0`; (4) join marketing distribution lists optional via lookup; (5) archive closed items quarterly for NCAs.

Step 2 — Create the search and alert
Run the following SPL in Search (then save as report or alert; adjust time range and threshold as needed):

```spl
index=itsm sourcetype="snow:sc_req_item" earliest=-365d
    (cat_item="*Product Governance*" OR cat_item="*MiFID Product*")
| eval signed_off=coalesce(u_target_market_signed_off, "false")
| eval is_closed=if(match(state,"(?i)closed|resolved|complete"),1,0)
| stats count as launches,
        sum(eval(is_closed=0)) as open_approvals,
        sum(eval(signed_off="false" AND is_closed=1)) as closed_without_signoff
    by cat_item
| where open_approvals>0 OR closed_without_signoff>0
| sort - open_approvals
```
